<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Get Divine Restriction: The Apex Hunt">
The journey to **Clover Retribution Divine Restriction** begins with the Apex boss. This mini-boss, often referred to as an "Apex boss," is your primary target. You must first possess the **Heavenly Restricted** trait and be at least level 250.

### Spawning Apex: The Bounty System

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Heavenly Restricted">
    Ensure your character has the **Heavenly Restricted** trait. This is a prerequisite for Apex to even consider putting a bounty on your head. Both Heavenly Restricted and Divine Restriction can be active simultaneously, even if the trait slot still displays Heavenly Restricted.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Hunt Mini-Bosses">
    Repeatedly kill mini-bosses or "Apex bosses" within the game. Each kill has a **10% chance** for Apex to place a bounty on you.
    <Callout type="info">
    Any enemy that tracks credit percentage is considered a boss. Fast rotations between Venom, Alpha Wolf, and Ogres are recommended. If you can't defeat Ogres quickly, stick to Venom and Alpha Wolf.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Await Apex's Arrival">
    Once you receive the message "Somebody has put a price on your head," Apex will begin hunting you. The time it takes for him to spawn depends on your current trait:
    - **Heavenly Restricted**: 10-20 minutes
    - **Divine Restriction**: 3-10 minutes (This reduced time makes farming significantly faster once you have the trait).
    <Callout type="warning" title="Crucial Warning">
    If you die or reset for any reason after the bounty message appears, Apex will not spawn. Additionally, you cannot be in a party when he hunts you, or he will immediately despawn.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Apex Boss Fight Strategies & Cheese Methods">
Apex is a formidable opponent, but with the right tactics, you can consistently defeat him, even solo. His attacks primarily consist of spear skills: Soaring Pierce, Swift Strike, and Down Slam, alongside M1 spam.

### Legitimate Combat Strategy

For a fair fight, the key is timing your dodges and blocks.
- **M1 Spam & Dodge**: Hold your M1 attack button and spam dodge after your fifth swing. This often guarantees an easy win, but be ready for his skills to occasionally break through.
- **Parrying**: If you're struggling with dodge timings (especially with Heavenly Restricted, which has fewer i-frames), parrying is a viable alternative. Hit block about a second after hitting him into a wall, just before he stands up.

<Callout type="success" title="Effective Combat Tip">
If you manage to stack bounties, multiple Apex bosses can hunt you at once, significantly speeding up your farming process. This is a rare but highly efficient way to get drops.
</Callout>

### Solo Cheese Method

This method is highly effective for solo players and ensures Apex gets stuck, making him easy to defeat.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Head to Timon Village">
    Locate the back house in Timon Village. This specific location is crucial for the cheese.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Position Yourself">
    Stand with your back to the wall of the back house. You want to be about a player's width away from the wall, facing away from it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Engage Apex">
    When Apex spawns, he will get stuck inside the wall. Immediately start attacking him with only M1s.
    <Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Common Mistakes">
    Do not walk all the way into the wall or step on the wooden frame at the bottom; this increases the chance of Apex getting out. The best method is to back away two steps after your fifth swing, then immediately walk back to the wall for your first swing to hit him.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Maintain Position">
    Keep an eye on Apex's position. If he starts moving to the left or right, adjust your camera in the opposite direction to correct his movement and keep him stuck. Being zoomed in helps with this.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Weapon Versatility">
Contrary to popular belief, this cheese method can be performed with any weapon, including swords. Proper positioning and timing are more important than weapon type.
</Callout>

### Group Cheese Method

If you have a friend, this method allows for easy Apex farming for drops and XP, though you cannot get Divine Restriction from someone else's Apex.

- **Inn Strategy (Timestone)**: Go to the upstairs rooms of the inn in Timestone. Have the player with the bounty jump onto the wall and face towards it. When Apex spawns, he will aggro only to the bounty holder. The other player can then punch Apex to death using M1s, avoiding spells that might hit the player on the wall.
- **Slanted Plank Method**: Less consistent, but can be used in emergencies. Find slanted wooden planks on taller buildings in Timestone. Jump onto the plank, walk up until just before you hit your head, and repeat the process.
