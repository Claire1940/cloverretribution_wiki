<Section icon={Swords} title="Legion Magic: Piece Breakdown and Scaling">

To effectively run a **Clover Retribution legion build**, you must understand the specific roles of each chess piece. Every piece drains mana over time, meaning your Intelligence stat doesn't just increase their power—it also increases your mana pool, allowing you to keep them active longer.

| Piece | Primary Function | Scaling Mechanic | Special Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pawn** | Single Target DPS/Heal | INT / Mastery | Attacks and heals the closest target simultaneously |
| **Rook** | AoE Damage / Shielding | 100% INT Scaling | Grants a shield equal to caster's INT to nearby allies |
| **Knight** | High Burst Damage | Base Mastery | Provides a **30% Damage Buff** to the nearest ally |
| **Bishop** | Elite Healing | High INT / Mastery | Casts AoE heals and high-level spells like Death Ward |

The Knight is widely considered the heavy hitter of the set, while the Rook is essential for party play due to its flat shield scaling. In a solo environment, a combination of the Pawn for consistent chip damage and the Bishop for survivability is often the most stable setup.

<Callout type="warning" title="Mana Depletion">
If your mana hits zero, all active Legion pieces will immediately disappear. Always keep an eye on your blue bar, especially when using the Bishop, which is the most expensive piece to maintain.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Prestige and Late-Game Optimization">

To take your **Clover Retribution legion build** to the absolute limit, you must engage with the Prestige system found at the Wizard King. Prestiging resets your level but provides permanent stat growth that is essential for tackling high-level bosses.

### Prestige Scaling Rewards
When you prestige at the current level cap (405), you receive significant bonuses:
- **Stat Points**: You gain 2.5% of your level as permanent stats. At level 405, this is approximately +10 stats per prestige.
- **Max Bonus**: You can prestige up to 5 times, resulting in a total of **+50 free stats** in every category (STR, DEX, INT, CON).
- **Four-Leaf Chance**: Prestiging at level 405 gives a base 10% chance to obtain a "Four-Leaf" version of your magic, which unlocks exclusive, powerful moves.

| Prestige Count | Total Bonus Stats | Four-Leaf Pity Chance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | +10 | 10% |
| 2 | +20 | 20% |
| 3 | +30 | 25% |
| 4 | +40 | 30% |
| 5 | +50 | 40% |

<Callout type="warning" title="The Prestige Boss">
Be aware that the Wizard King will eventually become a boss. The more levels you "feed" him through prestiging, the stronger he will be when you eventually have to face him in future updates.
</Callout>
