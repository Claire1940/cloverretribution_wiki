<Section icon={Target} title="Optimizing Your Magic Build">
Choosing the right magic is only the first step. To truly excel in Clover Retribution, you need to optimize your build around your chosen magic. This involves understanding stat priorities, trait synergies, and skill tree investments.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Understand Stat Scaling">
    Identify the primary stats that benefit your magic type (e.g., INT for most casters, STR/DEX for physical hybrids). Allocate your stat points accordingly to maximize damage or utility.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Invest in Skill Trees">
    Prioritize maxing out your magic's dedicated skill tree. Many magics gain significant power spikes or unlock crucial abilities through skill tree investment. This is especially true for magics like **Legion** and **Wind**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Synergize with Traits and Races">
    Select traits and races that complement your magic. For example, a **High Elf** (Legendary) with +10 INT and +15% Mana is perfect for magic users, while **Starborn** (Mythical) offers +65 INT and +50% Magic Resist. Look for traits that boost your specific damage type or provide mana regeneration.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Acquire Four Leaf Variants">
    If possible, aim for the Four Leaf variant of your chosen magic. These often provide additional abilities or significant buffs that elevate the magic's tier, as seen with **Balance** and **World Tree**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Adapt to the Meta">
    Stay updated with game patches and community discussions. The meta is constantly evolving, and what's best today might change tomorrow. Be prepared to adjust your build as new buffs or nerfs are introduced.
  </Step>
</Steps>
