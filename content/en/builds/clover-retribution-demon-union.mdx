<Section icon={Target} title="Initiating Your Demon Union Contract">
Before you can dive deep into the **Clover Retribution Demon Union**, you need to establish a contract with a devil. This process involves a quest and interaction with a key NPC.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Initial Quest">
    First, you must complete the quest titled "Somebody Has Gone Missing." This quest is a prerequisite for unlocking devil interactions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the NPC in Sand Village">
    After completing the quest, head to the Sand Village. An NPC there will have a silver or golden icon above their head, indicating they are the one you need to speak with.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Access the Devil Interaction Menu">
    Once you've spoken to the NPC, you'll gain access to the devil interaction menu. Here, you can choose to "speak to my own devil" if you've already obtained one.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Understand Contract Types">
    There are two primary contract types:
    - **Bound Contract:** You gain 50% of the devil's stats but must sacrifice EXP, Max HP, Soul Drain effect, or Gold.
    - **Unbound Contract:** You gain 80% of the devil's stats without sacrifice, but you must defeat the devil in a 1v1 battle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Contract Caution">
Choosing a Bound Contract locks you at 50% Devil Union. For maximum power, aim for an Unbound Contract by defeating the devil in combat.
</Callout>

It's essential to carefully consider which contract type best suits your playstyle and goals in Clover Retribution. The Unbound Contract offers greater power but requires a challenging confrontation.

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Devil Personalities and Union Progression">
Your devil's personality dictates the tasks you must complete to increase your **Clover Retribution Demon Union** percentage. Identifying your devil's personality is key to efficient progression. You can determine this by the text that appears on your screen while in Devil Union.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Arrogant Devil">
    - **Texts:** "You mustn't lose," "You don't need anyone else."
    - **Tasks:** Kill bosses solo (100% damage), cannot be in a party.
    - **Requirement:** Must be in Union Protection.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vengeful Devil">
    - **Texts:** "They can't stop us," "Forsake Mercy."
    - **Tasks:** Defeat players around your level, kill bosses alone.
    - **Recommendation:** Avoid parties for boss kills to be safe.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mysterious Devil">
    - **Texts:** "You must explore...," "Have you found me yet...?"
    - **Tasks:** Complete quests with a requirement over level 150 (e.g., Yetis, Scorpions, Nas), complete events (e.g., defeating Bandit Lord).
    - **Flexibility:** Can be in a party and damage percentage doesn't matter as long as quest/event is completed.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="info" title="Union Progression Mechanics">
Each boss kill or completed task for your specific devil type grants 1 point. You need 12 points to increase your Devil Union by 1%. Therefore, 120 points are required to reach 10% Union.
</Callout>

Remember, these tasks have a cooldown of approximately 10-13 minutes, during which no text will appear on your screen in Union. Plan your activities accordingly to maximize your progression.

</Section>
