The prestige mechanic allows you to trade your current level for spins. The formula generally provides spins based on your level divided by three, but the real power comes from the leveling process itself. For every two levels you gain, you receive one spin. By combining these methods, a dedicated player can cycle through thousands of spins in a single weekend of grinding.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level Cap">
    Grind to the current level cap (ideally 405) to maximize your pity and the base spin reward from the Wizard King's advisor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Level-Independent Gear">
    Ensure your main magic and key accessories are equipped. Some gear has level requirements that prevent re-equipping once you drop to level 1.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Prestige">
    Speak to Fabio (the advisor) and select "If only I had a few more chances" to reset your level in exchange for spins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Rapid Leveling Loop">
    Focus on high-XP targets like **Lucius** or the **Vampire Soldiers**. With magic builds, you can reach level 100+ in under 20 minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
