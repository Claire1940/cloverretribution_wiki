<Section icon={Target} title="Arcane Zenith's Impact on Mana Consumption">
Arcane Zenith provides a substantial **30% reduction in mana consumption**. This effect is a game-changer for magic-focused characters, as it translates directly into more spell casts over time. Compared to lower-tier mana-saving traits, Arcane Zenith offers a superior advantage, making it a highly sought-after legendary perk.

Consider the following comparison of mana consumption reduction traits:

| Trait Name     | Rarity   | Mana Consumption Reduction | Notes                                          |
| :------------- | :------- | :------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------- |
| **Arcane Adept** | Rare     | -10%                       | Entry-level mana efficiency.                   |
| **Mystic Savant**| Epic     | -20%                       | Improved mana efficiency, good mid-game option. |
| **Arcane Zenith**| Legendary| -30%                       | Top-tier mana efficiency, ideal for casters.   |

This table clearly illustrates the progression and superior benefit of Arcane Zenith. For players aiming to minimize mana costs, this trait is an indispensable component of their build.

<Callout type="success">
A 30% reduction means that for every 100 mana you would normally spend, you only spend 70. This allows for significantly longer combat engagements and fewer interruptions for mana recovery.
</Callout>
