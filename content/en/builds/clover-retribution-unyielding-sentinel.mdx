<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mechanics and Benefits of Unyielding Sentinel">
The core strength of **Unyielding Sentinel** lies in its scaling defensive bonus. Initially, it provides a respectable **+5% Passive DEF**. However, its true power activates when your character's health falls below 50%, at which point the defense bonus surges to **+25%**. This makes you incredibly resilient precisely when you need it most, turning potential defeat into a strategic advantage.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Health Threshold">
The enhanced defense from Unyielding Sentinel only activates when your HP is below 50%. Players must manage their health carefully to fully leverage this trait without being instantly defeated.
</Callout>

This mechanic encourages a playstyle where you can confidently engage enemies, knowing that your defenses will harden as the fight progresses. It's a game-changer for builds that thrive on sustained combat.
