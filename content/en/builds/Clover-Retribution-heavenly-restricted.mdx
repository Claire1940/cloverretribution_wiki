<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Heavenly Restricted and Divine Restriction">
In Clover Retribution, the **Heavenly Restricted** trait is a legendary status that fundamentally alters your character's combat style. Initially, it grants substantial physical advantages at the cost of your mana pool. However, its true potential is unlocked when it progresses to **Divine Restriction**, transforming your character into a physical powerhouse.

Initially, Heavenly Restricted provides:
- -80% Mana
- +15 Strength
- +15 Dexterity
- +15 Constitution
- +50% Bludgeoning, Slashing, and Piercing Damage

Upon achieving Divine Restriction, these benefits are dramatically amplified:
- 100% Mana reduction (weapon skills become mana-free)
- +20 Strength (total)
- +20 Dexterity (total)
- +15 Constitution
- +80% Bludgeoning, Slashing, and Piercing Damage (total)
- +20% Health
- +4 Speed
- Fall damage immunity
- Teleportation dashes (does not override Time Magic dashes)
- Ability to use Swordsman and Spearman class skills simultaneously

<Callout type="tip">
Divine Restriction is a game-changer for melee-focused builds, allowing for relentless physical attacks without worrying about mana costs for weapon skills.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Divine Restriction">
Unlocking **Divine Restriction** in Clover Retribution involves a specific set of prerequisites and a challenging boss encounter. This process ensures that only dedicated players can achieve this powerful trait.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level 250">
    Your character must be at least **Level 250**. This is a non-negotiable requirement before you can even begin the progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Heavenly Restricted for 8 Hours 20 Minutes">
    You must have the **Heavenly Restricted** trait equipped for a total of **8 hours and 20 minutes**. This duration is cumulative and does not require you to be actively in-game for the entire period.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger Apex Spawn">
    After meeting the level and time requirements, you need to trigger the spawn of **Apex**. This happens with a **10% chance** whenever you defeat a mini-boss (e.g., Venom, Ogres). A message will appear on your screen indicating that a bounty has been placed on your head.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat Apex">
    Once the bounty message appears, Apex will spawn behind you within 10-20 minutes if you are Heavenly Restricted. If you're already Divine Restricted, this time is reduced to 3-10 minutes. For your first successful progression, you must deal at least **30% of Apex's damage**. If you die, you can respawn him until you succeed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Rejoin Game (First Time Only)">
    After defeating Apex for the very first time (to progress your trait), you must **rejoin the game** for the trait to fully update. You'll know it's successful when your mana bar is completely empty.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Apex Encounter Tips">
- **Solo Strategy**: A common solo strategy involves positioning yourself with your back to a specific house in Timon Village. When Apex spawns inside the wall, you can attack him while he's bugged, preventing him from hitting you.
- **Group Strategy**: If playing with others, one player can stand on a door or house and block Apex's attacks while another player deals damage. This only works if both players have either Heavenly or Divine Restriction.
- **Party Despawn**: Be aware that Apex will instantly despawn if you are in a party when he spawns. It's best to go solo or with a specialized duo for the fight.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Apex Farming Strategies and Rewards">
Farming Apex is essential for acquiring the best gear for your **Divine Restriction** build. This mini-boss drops valuable items and tokens that are crucial for progression.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Triggering Apex">
    Regularly defeat mini-bosses like Venom or Ogres. Each kill has a 10% chance to trigger Apex's bounty. Look for the "Someone has placed a bounty on your head" message.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Solo Farming Spot">
    Head to Timon Village and find one of the specific houses. Stand with your back to the wall, facing the direction where Apex will spawn behind you. This often traps him within the house, allowing for easier solo kills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Group Farming (Divine Restricted Players Only)">
    If you're already Divine Restricted, you can farm Apex with another Divine/Heavenly Restricted player. One player can block Apex's attacks by standing on a high point (like a door or roof) while the other deals damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collecting Drops and Tokens">
    Apex has a 35% chance to drop an **Apex Token**. He also has a 2% chance each to drop the **Shroud of the Strongest** (armor), **The Forsaken Title**, and **Divine Nullifier** (sword).
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Exchanging Tokens">
    If luck isn't on your side, you can exchange Apex Tokens for these items. The merchant is located in a cave in the desert. Divine Nullifier (5 tokens), Shroud of the Strongest (5 tokens), The Forsaken Title (10 tokens).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">
The Forsaken Title is often the most important item to acquire from Apex Tokens due to its significant stat boosts, making it a priority for token exchange.
</Callout>
