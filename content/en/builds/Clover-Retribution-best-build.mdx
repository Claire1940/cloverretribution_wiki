<Section icon={Swords} title="The Divine Restriction (Toji) Build">

The Divine Restriction build is a high-investment, high-reward path that completely changes how you play. By sacrificing your ability to use magic (losing 100% mana), you gain incredible physical stats and unique abilities that make you a nightmare in both PvP and PvE.

    - **Survivability**: 20% flat Health increase and Fall Damage immunity.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Required Gear">

<Callout type="warning" title="Mana Sacrifice">
Once you progress to Divine Restriction, your mana bar is permanently removed. You cannot use any spells, so ensure you are committed to a weapon-based playstyle before completing the Apex trial.
</Callout>

### How to Unlock Divine Restriction
To achieve this build, you must first roll the **Heavenly Restricted** trait. This is a legendary trait that reduces your mana by 80% in exchange for base stat boosts.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level 250">
    You must hit the level cap of 250 and have held the Heavenly Restricted trait for at least 8 hours and 20 minutes of total time (not necessarily in-game).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trigger the Bounty">
    Kill high-level mobs like Venom until a message appears: "Someone has placed a bounty on your head." This indicates the Apex boss is coming.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat Apex">
    Apex will spawn behind you within 10-20 minutes. You must deal at least 30% of the damage to him. Rejoin the game after the kill to see your mana bar disappear and the trait evolve.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Stat Breakpoints">
Always invest at least one point into Intelligence early on to boost your mana regen. For end-game builds, aim for at least 300+ Intelligence to maximize the damage of high-tier spells like World Tree or Sunshine.
</Callout>

### Magic Synergy Ranking
Not all magics are equal for an Intelligence build. In 2026, the following magics dominate the meta:
1. **Sunshine**: 25% Damage Reduction and 15% more damage during the day.
2. **Sand**: Provides 15% flat Damage Reduction, making you surprisingly tanky.
3. **World Tree**: Offers shields and landmines, though it scales less aggressively than Sunshine.
