<Section icon={Swords} title="Progression and Tiering Up Your Hybrid Devil">
Advancing your Hybrid Devil through its tiers requires specific devil parts and the crafting of Infernal Trigons. This process is grind-intensive, but each tier unlocks greater power and customization.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Devil Parts">
    Collect **Devil Horns**, **Devil Eyes**, and **Devil Hearts** from normal devils and the world devil. Devil Horns provide 1 point, Devil Eyes 3 points, and Devil Hearts 10 points towards tier progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire Infernal Trigon Blueprint">
    The blueprint for the Infernal Trigon drops exclusively from normal devils. This item is crucial for efficient tier progression and has a 10% base drop chance to enter the epic drop pool.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Craft Infernal Trigons">
    At Alchemy Level 15, combine one Devil Heart, one Devil Eye, and one Devil Horn to craft an Infernal Trigon. Each Trigon provides 20 progression points and has a 5% chance to corrupt your Hybrid Devil.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Feed Your Hybrid Devil">
    Place the devil parts or crafted Infernal Trigons into your hotbar and use them to feed your Hybrid Devil. It's recommended to keep a manual record of your points, as there is no in-game tracker.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Progression Item | Points Gained | Corruption Chance |
|:-----------------|:--------------|:------------------|
| Devil Horn | 1 | 0% |
| Devil Eye | 3 | 0% |
| Devil Heart | 10 | 0% |
| Infernal Trigon | 20 | **5%** |

<Callout type="info" title="Tracking Progression">
Since there's no in-game counter for your Hybrid Devil's progression points, it's highly recommended to manually track how many points you've fed it. This prevents over-grinding and ensures efficient tier advancement.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Stat Allocation and Soul Consumption">
Hybrid Devils allow for extensive stat customization by consuming various 'Souls'. These souls directly contribute to your character's stats, but the total number of souls you can consume is limited by your Hybrid Devil's tier and corruption status.

| Soul Type | Primary Stat |
|:----------|:-------------|
| Soul of Wisdom | Intelligence |
| Soul of Fortitude | Constitution |
| Soul of Valor | Strength |
| Soul of Nimbleness | Dexterity |

Each soul consumed adds one point to its respective stat. The total number of souls you can consume is capped by your tier. For example, a Tier 1 Hybrid Devil can consume a maximum of 200 souls, while a Corrupted Tier 4 can consume up to 720.

**Recommended Stat Builds:**
- **Full Intelligence:** Ideal for magic-focused builds, maximizing spell damage and mana.
- **Constitution/Intelligence Mix:** Balances survivability with magical offense, suitable for certain magic types.

<Callout type="success" title="Stat Customization">
The Hybrid Devil's stat system allows for unparalleled customization. By strategically allocating souls, you can tailor your character's stats to perfectly complement your chosen magic and playstyle.

<Section icon={Target} title="Unlocking Hybrid Devil Magic">
Hybrid Devil Magic provides powerful abilities and passive bonuses, further enhancing your character. To unlock this feature, you must meet specific requirements and visit Fabio in the Devil's Hideout.

**Requirements to Unlock Devil Magic:**
- At least a **Tier 2 Corrupted** Hybrid Devil OR a **Tier 3** Hybrid Devil.
- **350 souls** sacrificed.
- Visit Fabio in the Devil's Hideout to roll for your Devil Magic.

| Available Devil Magics | Associated Passive | Notes |
|:-----------------------|:-------------------|:------|
| **Hellflame** | Nether Burn, Burn Immunity | Increased damage with Flame Magic. |
| **Curse** | Debuff Damage Boost, Debuff Immunity | More damage against debuffed enemies. |
| **Anti-Magic** | Damage Reduction, Player Mana Drain | Requires unlocking sword skills. |

Each Devil Magic comes with a unique passive ability that scales with your Hybrid Devil tier. These abilities provide significant combat advantages, from damage reduction to increased damage output under specific conditions.

<Callout type="info" title="Devil Mana">
Hybrid Devil Magics utilize a personal purple Devil Mana bar, located next to your HP. This mana regenerates passively, with regeneration speed increasing based on your Hybrid Devil tier.
