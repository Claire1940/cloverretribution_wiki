<Section icon={Shield} title="Building Your Ice Elf: Stats and Traits">
A well-rounded Ice Elf build requires careful allocation of stats and selection of beneficial traits. The goal is to create a character that can effectively utilize Ice Magic's support and control elements while maintaining survivability and contributing to overall team success.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize Intelligence">
    Allocate a significant portion of your stat points to **Intelligence**. This directly increases your magic damage, mana pool, and mana regeneration, which are all vital for an Ice Mage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Invest in Constitution">
    **Constitution** enhances your HP, providing much-needed durability, especially since Ice Magic often places you in a supportive role closer to the action. This allows you to withstand more hits while deploying your crowd control.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Consider Dexterity (Hybrid Elf)">
    If you choose a **Hybrid Elf**, investing in **Dexterity** can be beneficial, as it synergizes with the race's piercing and slashing damage bonus, offering a hybrid playstyle if desired. However, for pure Ice Magic, Intelligence and Constitution remain paramount.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Seek Mana-Boosting Gear">
    Look for accessories and armor that provide bonuses to your **Mana pool** or **Mana regeneration**. This ensures you can sustain your spellcasting throughout prolonged encounters without running out of resources.
  </Step>
</Steps>
