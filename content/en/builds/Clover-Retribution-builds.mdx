**Skill Tree Progression for Mages:**

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize Magic Damage Nodes">
    For Intelligence builds, invest in every skill tree node that grants magic damage. These are usually found in the "Int" skill tree branch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Consider Ethereion's Blessing Branch">
    This branch offers significant magic damage buffs for a relatively low skill point cost (14 SP). Access it by receiving Ethereion's blessing from a Spirit Rift.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Light Magic Users: Max Spirit Dive Branch">
    If you're a Light magic user, prioritize the Light Spirit Dive branch for a crucial 20% cooldown reduction, addressing a key bottleneck for the magic.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Devil Race: Focus on Devil Branch">
    Devil race players should allocate most skill points to the Devil branch, as it provides game-changing mechanics, including death avoidance. Pair with Dark Magic for an additional 20% magic damage buff.
