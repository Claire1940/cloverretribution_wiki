<Section icon={Swords} title="Sunshine Magic Spells and Abilities">
Sunshine Magic features a concise yet powerful set of spells, each contributing to its unique playstyle. Understanding these abilities is key to maximizing your effectiveness in combat.

### Bless Sun
This is your foundational buff. Activating Bless Sun grants you a significant boost, providing 10% more damage reduction and 10% increased damage. This buff scales with the number of clovers you possess, meaning the more clovers you have, the greater the benefits. It's an essential pre-combat ability for both offense and defense.

### Unmeasurable Heat
A powerful area-of-effect (AoE) spell, Unmeasurable Heat allows you to conjure a radiating heat blast. This spell deals damage over time to anyone caught within its radius. It's particularly effective for controlling choke points or damaging multiple enemies simultaneously. Note that Bless Sun must be deactivated to use this spell.

### Bless Flare
Bless Flare combines offensive projection with area denial. Upon casting, you launch a sun projectile that travels towards your target. Upon impact or reaching its destination, it creates an AoE circle on the ground, similar to Unmeasurable Heat, dealing damage over time to enemies within it. This spell offers excellent follow-up potential after engaging enemies.

### Passive Ability
Sunshine Magic also comes with a beneficial passive: you take less damage and deal more damage during the day. This environmental advantage further emphasizes the magic's theme and encourages strategic engagement based on the in-game time.

<Callout type="warning" title="Strategic Deactivation">
Remember to toggle off Bless Sun before casting Unmeasurable Heat. Failing to do so will prevent you from using Unmeasurable Heat, potentially costing you critical damage opportunities.
</Callout>

For player-versus-environment encounters, **Bless Sun** remains a strong opener, especially against tougher enemies. **Unmeasurable Heat** is excellent for clearing groups of mobs efficiently. **Bless Flare** can be used to initiate fights or finish off high-health targets. Focus on intelligent scaling to maximize your damage and survivability.
