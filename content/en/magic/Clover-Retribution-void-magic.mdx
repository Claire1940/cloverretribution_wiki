<Section icon={Target} title="The Gloom Mechanic and Passive Synergy">

The defining feature of **Clover Retribution void magic** is its passive ability. Every time a spell connects with an enemy, it applies the **Gloom** status effect. This isn't just a simple debuff; it is a stackable mechanic that transforms your combat loop.

**How Gloom Works:**
- Each hit applies one stack of Gloom.
- At **5 stacks**, a dark vortex manifests beneath the target's feet.
- The vortex deals continuous damage to the target and any nearby enemies.
- Gloom stacks also subtly reduce the target's movement speed, making them easier to trap in a **Depth Coil**.

| Build Component | Synergy Level | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Intelligence** | ★★★★★ | Increases DOT damage of the Gloom vortex. |
| **Constitution** | ★★★★☆ | Necessary to sustain the 300 mana cost of Void Zone. |
| **High Orc Race** | ★★★☆☆ | Provides the bulk needed to stand inside your own Void Zone. |
| **Elf Race** | ★★★★★ | Maximizes the mana pool and magic power for AOE scaling. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Strategy">
Use **Wormhole** to quickly apply the first stack of Gloom, then immediately follow up with **Depth Coil**. The slow from the initial stacks will keep the enemy trapped in the DOT cloud longer.
</Callout>
