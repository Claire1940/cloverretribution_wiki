<Section icon={Trophy} title="The Glacial Throne Mechanic">

The most unique aspect of **clover retribution ice magic** is the Glacial Throne. This ability does not just benefit the caster; it provides a strategic advantage to the entire party. When the throne is summoned, it presents a variety of weapons that can be picked up by anyone in the vicinity. This is particularly useful for players who may have lost their primary weapons or need a specific damage type for a boss phase.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Summon the Throne">
    Activate the Glacial Throne skill to spawn the ice weapon rack. Ensure you have sufficient mana, as the cooldown is relatively long.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear Your Hands">
    To pick up a weapon from the throne, you must unequip your current weapon. Interaction will fail if your hands are full.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Your Weapon">
    Walk up to the throne and interact with the desired weapon. The throne provides Greatswords, Spears, Staves, and Daggers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Engage Combat">
    Once equipped, these ice weapons provide unique movesets and scale with your magic stats, lasting for a set duration or until the throne expires.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The duration of the throne is approximately one minute, providing ample time for a full party to re-arm. In the 2026 updates, the weapons provided by the throne have seen a slight buff in their durability, making them viable for extended boss fights.

<Callout type="warning" title="Weapon Limitation">
You cannot pick up magic-based catalysts from the throne. It is strictly limited to physical-style weapons like swords and spears, though they still deal magical ice damage.
</Callout>
