<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Abilities: Spatial Barrage & Spatial Rapture">
While **Clover Retribution Spatial Magic** excels in utility, it also provides potent combat skills to engage enemies effectively. These abilities combine spatial manipulation with direct damage, offering unique attack patterns.

### Spatial Barrage

**Spatial Barrage** was one of the first offensive spatial moves introduced, firing unblockable projectiles.

| Ability           | Description                                  | Cooldown | Mana Cost | Mastery Required |
|-------------------|----------------------------------------------|----------|-----------|------------------|
| **Spatial Barrage** | Creates and fires unblockable spatial projectiles that deal damage on contact | 16 seconds | 150       | 75               |

This ability launches multiple projectiles, typically around 14 shots, dealing consistent damage. While good for initiation or adding pressure, it's generally not considered the primary damage source for a Spatial Magic build.

### Spatial Rapture

**Spatial Rapture** is a newer addition and significantly enhances Spatial Magic's combat viability.

| Ability           | Description                                  | Cooldown | Mana Cost | Mastery Required |
|-------------------|----------------------------------------------|----------|-----------|------------------|
| **Spatial Rapture** | Opens a portal to an alternate reality, summoning buildings to crash onto opponents | 30 seconds | 150       | Unknown          |

This ability has decent accuracy and can hit targets multiple times, making it a powerful tool in your arsenal. It's particularly effective for comboing with other abilities due to its reasonable cooldown.

<Callout type="warning" title="Damage Dependency">
Do not rely solely on Spatial Magic for heavy damage output. It's crucial to integrate Mage skills (like Mono Blast or Mono Burst) and intellect-boosting gear to maximize your combat effectiveness. Spatial Magic's cooldowns can be longer than other magic types, requiring careful management.
</Callout>
