### PvE Strategies
For player-versus-environment encounters, Water Magic offers strong utility against bosses and groups of enemies.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Engagement">
    Use **Water Dragon** to initiate combat from a distance, hitting multiple enemies and draining their mana. This can reduce their ability to cast powerful spells.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sustained Combat">
    Continuously use **Water Drill** on high-priority targets to keep their mana low, preventing them from using abilities. Maintain aggro if you're a tank, or focus on damage if you're a DPS.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defensive Phase">
    When the boss or enemies unleash powerful attacks, activate **Water Shield**. This will reduce incoming damage for your party and destroy projectiles, providing a crucial window for healing or counter-attacking.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Crowd Control">
    Against groups of adds or during boss phases with multiple targets, use **Maelstrom Surge** to group enemies together, making them vulnerable to AoE attacks from your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
