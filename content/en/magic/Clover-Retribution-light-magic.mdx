<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival and Mana Management">

The biggest hurdle for any **Clover Retribution light magic** user is the "Mana Wall." Even with high Intelligence, the ultimate spells drain resources faster than they can naturally replenish. 

### How to Fix Mana Issues

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Monsterous Reserves">
    This is a mythical trait that significantly increases your total mana pool and regeneration rate. It is almost mandatory for high-level Light gameplay.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Mana-Boosting Accessories">
    Look for earrings or capes that provide flat INT bonuses. Every point of INT slightly increases your mana regen per tick.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master the Rotation">
    Never lead with Light Pillar. Start with Holy Rain to soften targets, and only use the ultimate when the boss is stationary or stunned to ensure every tick of damage hits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Race Selection">
    If you cannot get Lunarian, consider **High Elf** or **Hybrid Elf**. These races offer natural mana pool bonuses that help bridge the gap if you lack the Monsterous Reserves trait.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Ultimate Drain">
Using two Light Pillars in rapid succession will put you in a "Bad State" with negative mana. Always keep at least 300 mana in reserve to cast a Light Dash for emergency positioning.
</Callout>
