### Detailed Skill Breakdown

**Lightning Strike** is your bread-and-butter opening move. By summoning a cloud directly above your target, you can initiate combat from a safe distance. The primary goal of this skill isn't just the initial damage, but the high chance to apply the **Shocked** debuff.

**Lightning Circus** acts as your primary crowd control and burst tool. When surrounded by multiple enemies, this skill creates a defensive and offensive perimeter of electricity. The synergy here is vital: if an enemy is already Shocked from your initial strike, the Circus sparks will deal significantly increased damage per tick.

**Thunder Gods Attire** is the definitive skill for Lightning users. At Mastery 75, your character becomes a literal bolt of lightning. This is not just a cosmetic change; your movement speed increases drastically, allowing you to kite bosses or chase down players in PvP. Furthermore, your basic M1 attacks gain electrical properties, making your physical combat just as lethal as your spells.

<Callout type="warning" title="Mana Management">

</Callout>
