<Section icon={Shield} title="Strategic Mastery: Shifting and Scaling">

The true power of **clover retribution balance magic** lies in the "Balance Shift" mechanic. This is not just a cosmetic change; it completely alters your role in a party. When the grimoire is in Purple mode, your abilities focus on "True Damage" and shield-breaking. In Yellow mode, you become the ultimate sustain machine.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Engagement">
    Start the fight in Purple (Damage) mode. Use Balance Budge to poke enemies and build up your mana pool while maintaining distance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Shield Breaking">
    If the enemy has a high-durability shield, activate True Balance. The damage pulses from Balance Magic are notorious for melting shields faster than traditional elemental spells.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Support Pivot">
    Once your health or your teammates' health drops below 50%, use Balance Shift. The transition is nearly instantaneous.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Burst Healing">
    Drop a Healing-mode Balance Budge at your feet. With high Intelligence, a single orb can restore over 70% of your HP in a matter of seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Mana Management">
While Balance Shift has no cooldown, True Balance consumes a significant portion of your mana bar. Ensure you have high Mana Regen stats through your gear or traits to maintain the pulsing field.
</Callout>

For 2026 builds, we recommend a 3:1 ratio of INT to VIT. This ensures your healing is potent enough to outpace incoming damage while keeping your health pool large enough to survive burst attacks from Shadow or Blood magic users.
