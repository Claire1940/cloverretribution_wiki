<Section icon={Swords} title="World Tree Magic: Nature's Wrath and Support">
World Tree Magic is a versatile Divine Magic type known for its ability to control the battlefield with roots and provide crucial support to allies. Its abilities often inflict the "Weakened" debuff, making enemies more vulnerable to damage. For Four-Leaf users, it unlocks a powerful healing and buffing spell.

<Callout type="tip">
World Tree Magic scales with your Intelligence (INT) stat. Higher Intelligence means more roots spawned for abilities like "World's Roots" and increased healing from "Great Tree Mistilteinn."
</Callout>

| Ability             | Mastery | Effect                                                                                                           | Debuff/Buff     |
| :------------------ | :------ | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :-------------- |
| **World's Roots**   | 10      | Summons roots in front of you, attacking enemies. Scales with INT (more roots with higher INT).                 | Weakened (chance) |
| **Arboreal Ascendancy** | 25      | Creates a root landmine at your cursor, damaging and rooting enemies when triggered.                             | Rooted (chance) |
| **Verdant Vortex**  | 45      | Summons a tree with a swirling whirlwind, dealing damage over time to enemies caught inside.                     | Damage over Time |
| **Forest's Embrace** | 60      | Unleashes an AOE burst of roots from the ground, damaging nearby enemies.                                         | Weakened, Rooted (chance) |
| **Great Tree Mistilteinn** | 75      | (Four-Leaf only) Spawns a giant tree that heals allies within its AOE and provides a shield.                     | Healing, Shield |

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Time Magic: Mastery Over Chronos">
Time Magic is a highly evasive and disruptive Divine Magic, offering players incredible mobility and control over combat flow. Its unique passives allow for dashes, dodges, and even a chance to revive after death, making it a formidable choice for players who prefer strategic positioning and survivability.

<Callout type="warning" title="Four-Leaf Requirement">
Some of Time Magic's most powerful abilities, like "Chrono Anastasis," are exclusive to Four-Leaf users. Without Four-Leaf, you'll miss out on significant damage-over-time potential.
</Callout>

| Ability             | Cooldown (s) | Effect                                                                                                           | Type         |
| :------------------ | :----------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :----------- |
| **Chrono Burst**    | 8            | Creates a time bubble around the target that explodes, dealing damage.                                           | Damage       |
| **Temporal Flick**  | 12           | Shoots three time projectiles that home in on the enemy.                                                         | Damage       |
| **Chrono Anastasis**| 65           | (Four-Leaf only) Summons a giant clock above you, dealing continuous damage over time to enemies within its radius. | Damage over Time |

**Passive Abilities:**
- **TP Dash:** Allows for quick, evasive movements with an afterimage effect.
- **Future Sight:** Grants a chance to automatically dodge incoming attacks when active.
- **Time Bar Revival:** Upon death, there's a chance to revive with a sliver of health if the time bar from the first move is active.

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Balance Magic: The Equilibrium of Combat">
Balance Magic offers a unique dual playstyle, allowing users to switch between offensive and defensive modes. This Divine Magic excels at adapting to various combat situations, providing either significant damage or potent healing depending on the user's strategic needs.

<Callout type="info">
Balance Magic is significantly more powerful with a Four-Leaf grimoire. While a three-leaf version is considered less effective, the Four-Leaf unlocks "True Balance," which amplifies the effects of "Balance Budge" in both damage and healing modes.
</Callout>

| Ability           | Mode       | Effect                                                                                                    | Notes         |
| :---------------- | :--------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------ |
| **Balance Budge** | Healing    | Creates an orb that heals allies within its radius.                                                       | Heals HP      |
| **Balance Budge** | Damage     | Creates an orb that damages enemies within its radius.                                                   | Deals Damage  |
| **Balance Shift** | N/A        | Toggles between Healing (yellow) and Damage (purple) modes.                                               | Mode Switch   |
| **True Balance**  | Healing    | (Four-Leaf only) Spawns a large AOE. When active, using Balance Budge in healing mode pulses significant healing. | AOE Healing   |
| **True Balance**  | Damage     | (Four-Leaf only) Spawns a large AOE. When active, using Balance Budge in damage mode pulses significant damage.   | AOE Damage    |

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Light Magic: Divine Illumination">
Light Magic is a powerful Divine Magic type that primarily focuses on support and area-of-effect damage. It's an excellent choice for players who enjoy contributing to team fights with healing and burst damage. Like other mythical magics, its effectiveness is amplified with a Four-Leaf grimoire.

<Callout type="success">
Light Magic's "Bright's Blessing" is a vital support ability for Four-Leaf users, providing significant healing to teammates within its area. This can turn the tide in challenging encounters.
</Callout>

| :---------------- | :------ | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :-------------- |

| **Light Pillar**  | 25      | Summons a powerful pillar of light, dealing high damage in an area.                                              | AOE Damage      |
| **Divine Burial** | 45      | Jumps up and fires a massive beam of light, dealing immense damage.                                              | Burst Damage    |
| **Bright's Blessing** | 75      | (Four-Leaf only) Creates a healing circle that restores health to allies within its radius.                      | AOE Healing/Support |

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Sunshine Magic: Radiant Power">
Sunshine Magic is a formidable Divine Magic that offers both defensive buffs and potent offensive capabilities, particularly with its area-of-effect damage. It rewards aggressive playstyles while also providing a layer of survivability.

<Callout type="tip">
Sunshine Magic has a passive that grants damage reduction and increased damage during the day, making it strategically advantageous to fight during daylight hours.
</Callout>

| Ability           | Effect                                                                                                      | Notes                                                              |

| **Unmeasurable Heat** | Conjures an AOE heat blast that deals damage over time to enemies within its radius.                      | Requires "Bless Sun" to be off. Good for sustained AOE damage.    |
| **Bless Flare**   | Shoots a sun projectile that creates an AOE damage-over-time circle upon impact.                          | High burst damage with residual AOE.                               |
