<Section icon={Target} title="Building Your Shadow Magic Character">
Crafting an effective Shadow Magic character involves strategic stat allocation and skill point distribution. Here’s a breakdown of key considerations:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Stat Prioritization">
    As a magic user, **Intelligence (INT)** is your paramount stat. It directly scales the damage of all your Shadow Magic spells. After INT, consider **Constitution (CON)** for increased survivability, as Shadow Magic can involve close-quarters combat with its domain effects. Dexterity (DEX) and Strength (STR) are less critical for a pure magic build.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Skill Point Acquisition">
    Skill points are primarily obtained through promotion quests at the Wizard King and completing missions from the mission board at the Coliseum. You'll need to reach level 500 to prestige and potentially reset skill points.
    For more details on promotions and skill points, check out this general update overview: [Clover Retribution Update Info](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jk5KvJpc-MU).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Recommended Skill Tree Progression">
    Start by investing in **Umbral Affinity** for immediate damage gains. Next, max out **Coffin Spires** to empower your Night Coffin. Then, consider **Volatile Detonation** for Shadow Node burst and **Phantom Network** for advanced AoE combos. Survivability nodes like **Well of Night** can be picked up later based on your playstyle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Gear and Grimoire Selection">
    Aim for gear that provides Intelligence bonuses. When spinning for grimoires, remember that mastery does not save between different magics unless you have the Clover Storage gamepass. Focus on mastering Shadow Magic to unlock all its spells.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Skill Point Reset">
If you wish to reset your skill points, you must reach the new level cap of 500 and then prestige by visiting the Wizard King. This allows for build experimentation.
</Callout>

Shadow Magic offers a dynamic and rewarding playstyle, especially for those who enjoy high burst damage and tactical area control.

</Section>
