<Section icon={Swords} title="Chaos Bomb: Unleashing Explosive Surprises">
Chaos Bomb is your primary offensive ability when wielding Dice Magic. Upon activation, you roll a dice, and the outcome directly influences the number and type of bombs spawned. This makes it an excellent tool for both area denial and direct damage, keeping your opponents constantly guessing.

The varying bomb counts can be used to zone enemies, interrupt their actions, or simply overwhelm them with explosions. The largest bomb, triggered by higher rolls, also provides a significant knockback, creating opportunities for follow-up attacks or escapes.

### Chaos Bomb Dice Roll Effects

| Dice Roll | Effect                                      | Impact                                           |
| :-------- | :------------------------------------------ | :----------------------------------------------- |
| **4-6**   | Create **3 bombs** that track and explode.  | Good for single targets or small groups.         |
| **7-9**   | Create **6 bombs** that track and explode.  | Increased area coverage and damage potential.    |
| **10-12** | Spawn **1 large bomb** with knockback.      | Excellent for crowd control and interrupting.    |

<Callout type="warning">
Be mindful of your surroundings when using Chaos Bomb. The bombs can be a bit buggy and might not always target exactly as intended, leading to unexpected outcomes or friendly fire in group settings.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Miracle Tonic: The Gambler's Healing Touch">
Miracle Tonic is the defensive and utility counterpart to Chaos Bomb, offering a range of healing and crowd control effects. Just like Chaos Bomb, its outcome is determined by a dice roll, adding an element of chance to your survival and support capabilities.

From minor health boosts to full healing and enemy stunning, Miracle Tonic can be a lifesaesaver in tight situations. The speed buff from higher rolls can also be crucial for escaping dangerous encounters or closing in on a fleeing enemy.

### Miracle Tonic Dice Roll Effects

| Dice Roll | Effect                                            | Benefit                                             |
| :-------- | :------------------------------------------------ | :-------------------------------------------------- |
| **1-3**   | Heal **15% max HP**, blind opponents for **2s**. | Minor heal, useful for disengage or quick CC.       |
| **4-6**   | Heal **25% max HP**.                              | Decent burst heal for sustained combat.             |
| **7-9**   | Heal **all HP**, stun enemies for **3s**.         | Game-changing, full recovery and strong CC.         |
| **10-12** | Gain **25% HP**, **+7% walk speed** for **12s**.  | Temporary boost for offense or escape, followed by nerf. |
