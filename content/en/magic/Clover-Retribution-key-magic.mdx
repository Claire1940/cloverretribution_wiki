<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Understanding Clover Retribution Key Magic">
**Key Magic** in Clover Retribution is a highly sought-after Divine magic type that grants players the unique ability to **copy spells** from other players. This mechanic introduces an incredible layer of versatility, allowing users to adapt their toolkit to almost any combat situation, making it a formidable choice for both solo play and team roles. Unlike other magic types, Key Magic's power lies not in its inherent spells, but in its capacity to mimic and enhance the abilities of others.

**Key Magic** users begin with three arcane doors (slots) and gain a fourth with a Four-Leaf grimoire. Each slot applies distinct modifications to the copied spells, influencing their mana consumption and cooldowns. Mastering these slot effects and strategically choosing which spells to copy is paramount to unlocking Key Magic's full potential.

- Key Magic is a Divine rarity magic, highly valued for its copying abilities.
- Players need sufficient magic mastery to copy spells, matching the copied spell's requirement.
- Four-Leaf Key Magic grants an additional, highly advantageous fourth slot.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Key Magic Slots and Their Effects">
Key Magic features unique "Arcane Doors" or slots, each applying a different modifier to the copied spell. Understanding these effects is vital for strategic spell placement and optimizing your build.

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Mastery Note">
You can only copy spells for which you meet the required **magic mastery**. If a spell requires 150 mastery, your Key Magic must also be at least 150 mastery to copy it. You also cannot copy another Key Magic or Imitation Magic user.
</Callout>

| Arcane Door (Slot) | Effect on Copied Spell                               | Strategic Use                                                                |
| :----------------- | :--------------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Slot 1**         | -25% Mana Cost, +300% Cooldown                      | **Buffs** (e.g., Blessed Sun), **High Cooldown Ultimates** (e.g., Dimension Slash, Final Slash) |
| **Slot 2**         | -25% Cooldown, +300% Mana Cost                      | **Low Cooldown, High Mana Efficiency** abilities (e.g., Dark Slash, Water Bubble) |
| **Slot 3**         | +200% Mana Cost, +200% Cooldown (unlocked at 150 mastery) | **Situational Spells** or those with naturally low base costs/cooldowns (e.g., Self-Heal, Healing Hands, Thunder God's Attire) |
| **Slot 4**         | -25% Mana Cost, -25% Cooldown (Four-Leaf exclusive) | **Strongest Abilities**, primary damage dealers, or critical utility (e.g., Dark Hole, Holy Rain) |

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Optimizing Your Key Magic Build">
Building an effective **Clover Retribution Key Magic** setup requires careful consideration of copied spells, race, traits, families, and accessories. The goal is to create synergy that maximizes your chosen role, whether it's burst damage, support, or tanking.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Clover Upgrades">
    **Crucial Investment:** Upgrading your Key Magic slots with clovers to the max significantly increases **attack potency**. This is almost as challenging as clovering Legion Magic but provides immense benefits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No Passives">
    Copied spells do **not transfer passive abilities**. For instance, a copied Dark ability will deal less damage than if used by a true Dark Magic user.
  </Card>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Strategic Spell Selection">
    Prioritize spells based on the slot effects. For example, **Blessed Sun** (Sunshine) is ideal for Slot 1 due to its continuous mana consumption and buffing nature. **Dark Hole** and **Holy Rain** are excellent choices for the potent Slot 4.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mana Management">
    Given the mana cost variations, consider incorporating mana regeneration spells or traits like **Monstrous Reserves** if your build is mana-heavy, especially with Legion abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Supportive Combinations">
    For support roles, combining **Blessed Sun** (Slot 1) for team buffs with a healing spell or **Melody Blast** (Slot 3) for teammates can create a powerful utility build.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Damage Combos">
    Consider multi-hit abilities like **Thunder God's Attire** and **Depth Coil** for synergistic damage output. The versatility of Key Magic allows for numerous powerful combinations.
  </Step>
</Steps>
