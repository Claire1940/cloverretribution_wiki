<Section icon={Target} title="Blood Magic Abilities & Usage">
**Clover Retribution Blood Magic** offers a diverse set of abilities, ranging from close-range slashes to tracking projectiles and area-of-effect (AoE) attacks. Each ability scales with your clovers, making continuous progression vital for maximizing damage.

Magic mastery does not save between grimoires unless you own the **Clover Storage gamepass**. If you switch away from Blood Magic and then return, your mastery progress will be reset without this gamepass.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="PvP and PvE Strategies with Blood Magic">
**Clover Retribution Blood Magic** excels in both player-versus-player and player-versus-environment combat due to its versatile kit and high damage potential, especially with a Four-leaf Grimoire. Understanding how to effectively use your abilities and manage your resources is key to success.

<Callout type="info" title="Mana Management">
Always have **Mana Burst** equipped. It acts as an evasive move, allowing you to escape combos even while being hit, which is crucial for survival in intense fights.
</Callout>

**PvP Tactics:**
- **Aggression**: Blood Magic allows for aggressive play. Continuously pressure opponents with your moves.
- **Combo Extension**: Use **Wound** to extend combos or initiate them.
- **Guard Break**: **Crimson Cataclysm** is vital for breaking opponents' guards, opening them up for further attacks.
- **Tracking**: **Crimson Pursuit** provides ranged pressure and can track evasive opponents.
- **Hyper Armor**: Your roar ability (if applicable to your build) provides hyper armor or i-frames, making you immune to damage during its animation.
- **Evasion**: Master **Mana Burst** to escape tight situations and avoid taking heavy damage.

**PvE Tactics:**
- **Boss Farming**: With a strong build, you can often defeat lesser golems and other bosses in a single cycle.
- **Crimson Hydra**: For Four-leaf users, summon the **Crimson Hydra** early in boss fights to maximize its damage output. It provides consistent pressure and can knock back enemies.
- **Tankiness**: The recommended CON-heavy build makes you very tanky, allowing you to withstand hits from most PvE enemies without significant threat.
- **M1 Spam**: Combine your abilities with rapid M1 (basic attack) inputs for continuous damage.
