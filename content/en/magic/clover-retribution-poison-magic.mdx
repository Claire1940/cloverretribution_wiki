<Section icon={Trophy} title="Steps to Obtain and Master New Magic">
Acquiring new magic in Clover Retribution involves spinning for grimoires, which can be obtained through Robux purchases or redemption codes. Once you have a suitable magic, mastering it is key to unlocking its full potential.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Spins">
    You can get spins by purchasing them with Robux or by redeeming codes. Keep an eye out for in-game events or official announcements for potential free spin opportunities. Access the 'Spin' button from the main menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Spin for Grimoires">
    Head to the spinning interface and roll for a new magic type. Magics have varying rarities, from Common to Mythical, with rarer magics generally offering better performance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check for Four-Leaf Grimoires">
    There's a 1% chance of spinning a grimoire with '4 Leaf' in brackets. This unlocks an exclusive four-leaf move for that specific magic, significantly enhancing its power.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Invest in Intelligence (INT)">
    All magic in Clover Retribution scales with your Intelligence stat. Prioritize leveling up your INT to boost your magic damage and effectiveness.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Gain Magic Mastery">
    Use your chosen magic frequently to gain mastery. As your mastery increases, you will unlock new spells and abilities specific to that grimoire.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Consider Clover Storage Gamepass">
    If you plan to experiment with multiple magic types, the Clover Storage gamepass is invaluable. It saves your magic mastery and four-leaf spins across rerolls, allowing you to switch between grimoires without losing progress.
  </Step>
</Steps>
