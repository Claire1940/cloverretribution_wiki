<Section icon={Target} title="The Vampire Lord Lucius Boss Fight">

Defeating Lucius is mandatory to obtain the **Pure Vampire Blood**, a mythical item required for race evolution. Lucius has two forms: Normal and Corrupted. The Corrupted version is identified by a purple aura and a double system message ("A shadow deepens..."). 

**Combat Mechanics:**
- **Normal Lucius**: Standard attacks and a shield phase.
- **Corrupted Lucius**: No shield below 50% HP, but significantly higher damage output and aggressive AoE attacks.
- **AoE Warning**: Watch for white signs on the floor; move immediately to avoid the high-damage blast.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Clear the Lackeys">
    Kill 12 Vampire Soldiers around the castle bridge to trigger the "Stronger vampires can be seen" message.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defeat the Elites">
    Follow the purple dot on your compass to find the three Elite Vampires. Killing them triggers Lucius's awakening.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Cheese Strategy">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Infection and the Scarlet Affliction">

Once you have the Pure Vampire Blood, you cannot simply consume it to become an Elder Vampire immediately. You must first be "infected." This involves finding a player who has already reached the **Awakened Elder Vampire** stage.

**The Infection Process:**
1. Find an Awakened Elder Vampire player (check the official Discord).
2. Have them use their transformation and bite you.
3. There is a **5% chance** for a successful infection.
4. If successful, you will receive the **Scarlet Affliction** trait for 24 hours.
5. If you die and see red text, the infection failed; you must wait for the daily reset (12 hours, or 6 hours with Golden Clover) to try again.

| Infection Outcome | Probability | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Failed Infection** | 95% | Character dies/screams in pain |
| **Scarlet Affliction** | 5% | Required trait for race change |
| **Pure Blood Usage** | 100% | Consumes item to change race |
