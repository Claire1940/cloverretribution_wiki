In contrast, Blood Magic users often rely on the **Crimson Torrent** spell, which provides excellent single-target damage and healing but lacks the team-wide utility of a Legion Bishop.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Prestige and Guild Systems">

The 2026 updates introduced the Prestige system, a long-term progression mechanic that rewards dedicated players. By speaking to the Wizard King, you can reset your progress for permanent benefits.

**Prestige Rewards and Scaling:**
- **Stat Boosts**: Receive 2.5% of your level as permanent stats (Con, Int, Str, Dex). At the level 405 cap, this is approximately 10 stats per prestige.
- **Four-Leaf Clover Chance**: Scaling chance to obtain a four-leaf clover for your magic. Starts at 10% (at level 405) and increases by 10% per failed attempt (up to 40%).
- **Spins**: Receive your level divided by three in spins (approx. 133 spins at level 405).
