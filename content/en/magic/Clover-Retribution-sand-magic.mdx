Sand Magic scales effectively with intelligence. ### PvE and Boss Strategies
For PvE content and boss fights, Sand Magic offers great utility. Sandstorm's slow and damage-over-time can continuously chip away at boss health, while Sand Swipe provides consistent, low-cooldown damage. Sand Tsunami can be used for crowd control against smaller enemies or to add burst damage. The Sand Shield is invaluable for soaking up powerful boss attacks, allowing you to sustain through challenging encounters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Engagement">
    Activate **Sandstorm** to apply slow and initial damage, creating an aura around you.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sustained Damage">
    Spam **Sand Swipe** due to its low cooldown and good hitbox for consistent damage output.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defensive Stance">
    Deploy **Sand Shield** when the boss prepares a heavy attack or when your health is low, absorbing significant damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Crowd Control/Burst">
    Use **Sand Tsunami** to drag groups of enemies or to add a burst of damage to bosses, especially when they are vulnerable.
  </Step>
</Steps>
