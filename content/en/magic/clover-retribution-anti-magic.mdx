<Section icon={Map} title="Locating and Identifying Anti-Magic Rifts">

Finding an anti-magic devil is the first major hurdle for any aspiring player in 2026. Unlike Hellfire or Cursed devils, which dominate the spawn rates, anti-magic is a rare occurrence that requires patience and often the use of specialized items. When a Devil Rift appears, the game rolls a probability check to determine which entity resides within.

| Devil Type | Spawn Chance | Primary Attribute |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hellfire** | 60% | Fire Pillars / Burst Damage |
| **Cursed** | 35% | Clones / Confusion |
| **Anti-Magic** | 5% | Mana Drain / Sword Rain |

Before entering, you must identify the Tier of the devil. Tier 1 and 2 devils are significantly easier and suitable for farming drops. However, if you are looking to secure a contract, you must face the Tier 3 or 4 variants. These are identified by the **black lightning** flickering around the rift's entrance. If the rift lacks this lightning, you are facing a lower-tier opponent.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Anti-Magic Devil Boss Mechanics">

The fight against an anti-magic devil is a test of endurance and positioning. Every devil follows a strict cycle: an **Attack Phase** where they are immune to all damage, followed by a **Stun Phase** lasting approximately 11 seconds. During the stun phase, you must unleash your maximum DPS before the devil resumes its assault.

### Tier 1-2 Attack Patterns
Lower-tier devils utilize two primary sword attacks. The **Demon Slayer Swords** drop in increments of two—one targeting your exact location and one dropping randomly. Simultaneously, **Demon Destroyer Swords** spawn across the arena. These swords are particularly dangerous for mages as they drain 50-70 mana upon spawning and deal 100 damage per tick in a lingering AoE.

### Tier 3-4 Attack Patterns
High-tier fights escalate the chaos significantly. You will face upwards of 35 Demon Slayer swords dropping in rapid, random successions. The most lethal addition is the **Giant Slash**, a wide-reaching arena attack with a 3-second wind-up.

| Attack Name | Tier | Effect | Counter |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Demon Slayer Sword** | 1-4 | Direct physical damage | Perfect Dodge or Parry |
| **Demon Destroyer Sword** | 1-4 | **50-70 Mana Drain** / AoE tick | Stay out of range |
| **Giant Slash** | 3-4 | Massive arena-wide damage | Dash out of range / I-frames |
| **Black Lightning** | 3-4 | High damage / Stun | Constant movement |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Anti-magic attacks scale with the devil's **Intelligence (INT)** stat. If you encounter a high-INT Tier 4 devil while playing a Mage build, the mana drain and damage will likely end the fight instantly. Always check the devil's aura before committing.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimal Build: The Divine Restriction Advantage">

While it is technically possible to defeat an anti-magic devil using high-tier magic like **World Tree** or **Void**, it is highly discouraged for the average player. The anti-magic mechanics are specifically designed to punish mana-reliant characters. This makes the **Divine Restriction (DR)** build the undisputed meta for this encounter in 2026.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Tankiness">
    DR builds offer significantly higher base health and damage reduction compared to glass-cannon mages.
  </Card>
  <Card title="I-Frames">
    The dodges in a Divine Restriction build have **insane range** and longer invincibility frames.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mana Immunity">
    Since DR builds do not rely on traditional mana for primary damage, the Demon Destroyer's drain is less impactful.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

If you insist on using Magic, you must master the **Parry** mechanic. Many players are unaware that Demon Slayer swords can be parried. The window is surprisingly generous, and successful parries can keep a mage alive long enough to reach the stun phase. However, one mistake usually results in a complete mana wipe.

<Callout type="success" title="Build Recommendation">
Pair Divine Restriction with **Elder Vampire** or **Ryoman** regeneration. These passive heals work during the fight if you stay out of combat for 15-30 seconds, allowing you to reset your health between attack cycles.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Unlocking Anti-Magic Spells and Abilities">

Securing a contract with an anti-magic devil is only the beginning. To truly harness its power, you must progress through **Devil Union Mastery**. Unlike Hellfire devils, which grant the purplish flame buff, anti-magic focuses on physical dominance and weapon enhancement.

| Ability | Requirement | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Anti-Magic Greatsword** | 25% Union Mastery | Summons a massive blade for melee combat. |
| **Anti-Magic Whirlwind** | Greatsword Active | A spinning AoE attack that clears nearby enemies. |
| **Mana Shielding** | Passive | Reduces incoming magical damage based on Union level. |
| **World Slasher** | Divine Restriction | High-damage projectile slash (Requires DR build). |

To obtain these spells, you have a **75% chance** to receive a new ability each time you defeat your devil during a "Training" session. Note that devil training can only be performed every two hours and the devil becomes progressively stronger with each victory.

<Callout type="info" title="Mastery Note">
Devil Union progress is separate from your standard Magic Mastery. You must actively use your devil form or complete training to increase this percentage.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Binding Vows and Relationship Management">

Your relationship with your devil determines how quickly you can access training and binding vows. Unbound devils start at a neutral 0 relation, while bound devils—those you have made a contract with—start at a deficit of -20. You must bridge this gap through quests and meditation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Meditation">
    Press **N** to meditate for 10 minutes. This allows you to enter your mindscape and talk to your devil.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Relationship Quests">
    Complete the **Mana Wisp quest** (+5 relation), **Eyeball quest** (+10-15), or the **Devil Heart quest** (+20).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Conversational Choices">
    When talking to your devil, choose "Mindlessly bicker" to answer personality-based questions for easy relation points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Binding Vows">
    Once your relation is high enough, choose "I want to get stronger" to initiate a Binding Vow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Binding Vow Options
There are currently two major deals you can strike. Be warned: making a vow will cause **Spirits** to reject you for a significant period.

| Vow Type | Requirement | Benefit | Drawback |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mana Exchange** | All Users | +10% Devil Mana | -10% Total Regular Mana |
| **The Sacrifice** | Unbound Only | +5% Permanent Devil Stats | Permanent loss of one arm |

<Callout type="warning" title="Visual Bug">
The "Sacrifice" vow removes your character's arm. While this provides a permanent stat boost, the visual effect can sometimes clip through armor sets. This is a known cosmetic issue in the current 2026 build.
</Callout>
