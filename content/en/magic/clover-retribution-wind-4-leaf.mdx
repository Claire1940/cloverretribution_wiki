<Section icon={Map} title="Summoning Shenron and Making the Wish">
Once you have successfully acquired a Corrupted Dragon Ball, the next step involves summoning Shenron and making the specific wish to evolve your Grimoire. This is a critical moment in upgrading your **Clover Retribution Wind 4 Leaf** Grimoire.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Corrupted Artifact Spawn">
    Head to the specific corrupted artifact spawn location in the game world. This is where Shenron can be summoned using a Corrupted Dragon Ball.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Summon Shenron">
    Interact with the corrupted artifact while possessing a Corrupted Dragon Ball to summon Shenron. A prompt will appear to initiate the summoning.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Choose the Grimoire Evolution Wish">
    Upon summoning Shenron, you will be presented with a list of wishes. Select the option that allows you to evolve your Grimoire into its 4-leaf form. It will typically be phrased as "Evolve your Grimoire into a 4-leaf."
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm Evolution">
    Confirm your wish. Your current 3-leaf Grimoire will then be upgraded to a powerful 4-leaf Grimoire. This will apply to any type, including your Wind Grimoire.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Wish Confirmation">
