<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Shadow Magic Deep Dive">

Shadow Magic is the crown jewel of the latest update. It is a sub-type found within the Divine Pool, making it one of the rarest pulls in the game. Unlike other magics, Shadow currently lacks a 4-Leaf variant, but its base spells are powerful enough to compensate.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtaining Shadow">
    Roll in the Divine Pool. You have a 25% chance to hit the Shadow sub-pool, resulting in a total 0.0075% chance per spin.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Spells">
    Reach Magic Mastery level 10 to unlock all four current spells. There is no level requirement for the first spell.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mastering Spells">
    Focus on the Night Coffin spell. It creates a pool of shadows that repeatedly burst for thousands of damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimizing Stats">
    Currently, Shadow spells have poor stat scaling. It is recommended to go 100% Constitution (CON) for survivability while relying on base spell damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Spell Name | Effect Description | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shadow Node** | Summons a ball for teleportation | Mobility & Positioning |
| **Domain** | Summons shadow clones that burst | Area Denial |
| **Umbral Doppelganger** | Homing shadow clones | PvP & Mobile Targets |
| **Night Coffin** | Repeated shadow bursts | High DPS Bossing |

<Callout type="success" title="Shadow Coffin Synergy">

The Skill Tree system is the newest layer of customization for your magic. To access the best nodes, you must climb the ranks of the Magic Knights. This requires earning XP and completing promotion quests from the mission board located in Clover Capital.

Each promotion rewards you with Skill Points (SP). These points are used to unlock powerful modifiers for your magic, such as increased burn damage for Flame or faster attack speed for Legion.

| Rank Category | Levels | Promotion Cooldown |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Junior** | 1 - 4 | 26 - 44 minutes |
| **Intermediate** | 1 - 4 | 50 - 68 minutes |
| **Senior** | 1 - 4 | 74 - 92 minutes |
| **Grand** | 1 - 4 | 98 - 116 minutes |

<Callout type="warning" title="Item Requirements">
Certain high-tier skill tree nodes require rare items like the Tempest Heart (for Wind) or Spirit Feathers. These have a low drop rate from Mission Board orders, so start farming them early.
