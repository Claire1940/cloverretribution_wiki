### Hammer: The Unstoppable Force
Hammer is considered one of the best weapon classes, especially for PvP, due to its incredibly powerful skills that all come with hyper armor. This means you cannot be interrupted while using them, making you an absolute menace in close-quarters combat.

### Spear: The Consistent Damage Dealer
Spear is a fantastic choice for consistent damage, especially in PvE. Its unique M1 mechanics allow for dodging between attacks, making it highly adaptable and survivable.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Mastering M1 Dodges">
    The ability to dodge mid-M1 combo is a core strength. Practice timing your dodges to maintain damage output while avoiding incoming attacks. This is crucial for surviving tougher PvE encounters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilizing Skills for Combos">
    The first two spear skills are excellent for extending combos, particularly in PvP. While its M1 range can be challenging in PvP, landing a full combo can be devastating.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="PvE Dominance">
    Spear shines in PvE as a consistent damage dealer. Its M1 speed and dodge capability allow for sustained engagement against various enemies without breaking your attack flow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Staff: Ranged Combat Specialist
Staff is a versatile weapon class known for its excellent range and a surprisingly powerful M2 attack. While its M1s might lack damage, its skills offer good utility and burst.

<Callout type="info">
The Staff's M2 attack is considered one of the best in the game for both PvP and PvE due to its incredible range and damage. Focus on utilizing this ability effectively.
</Callout>
