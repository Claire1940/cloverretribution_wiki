<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Race Selection Strategy: A Step-by-Step Guide">
Choosing your race wisely can set the foundation for a powerful character. Follow these steps to make the best decision for your playstyle.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Define Your Playstyle">
    Before selecting a race, determine if you prefer **magic-focused combat**, **melee damage**, **tanking**, or **support/utility**. Your playstyle will heavily influence which race benefits you most. For example, mages should prioritize INT and Mana, while melee fighters need STR and CON.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Consult the Tier List">
    Refer to the **Clover Retribution race tier list** to identify top-performing races within your desired playstyle. Look for races in SS, S, and A tiers that align with your build goals. Pay attention to the specific stat bonuses and unique traits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Consider Rarity and Availability">
    Mythical and Legendary races often offer the best stats and abilities but are harder to obtain. If you're starting, don't shy away from strong Epic or even Rare races that can carry you through early-game content. You can always aim for higher-tier races later.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evaluate Synergies with Magic and Traits">
    A race's full potential is unlocked when it synergizes well with your chosen magic and traits. For instance, a Dark Elf pairs exceptionally with fire magic, and a Dryad enhances healing spells. Research potential combinations to maximize your character's effectiveness.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Plan for Progression">
    Some races, like Dreadscale, offer significant benefits through evolution or progression systems. Factor in the long-term growth potential of a race. A race that starts strong might not scale as well as one with a robust progression path.
  </Step>
</Steps>
