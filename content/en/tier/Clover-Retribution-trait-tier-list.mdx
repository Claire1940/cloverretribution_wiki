<Section icon={Zap} title="How to Optimize Your Traits">

Optimizing your character in Clover Retribution requires a balance between your Race, Magic, and Trait. A common mistake is picking a trait that contradicts your Race's natural strengths. For example, picking Heavenly Restricted on a High Elf (a race built for magic) wastes the race's core potential.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Your Build Path">
    Decide if you are going for **Pure Mage**, **Pure Melee**, or a **Hybrid**. This choice dictates which traits are "S-Tier" for you specifically.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Level Up with Prodigy">
    If you are below the level cap, prioritize getting **Prodigy**. The 100% XP boost will save you dozens of hours of grinding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reroll for End-Game">
    Once maxed, use your saved spins to target **Monstrous Reserves** (Mage) or **Heavenly Restricted** (Melee).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Synergize with Race">
    Pair **Ryomen** or **Mahari** races with **Adaptive Resistance** for the ultimate tank build, or **High Elf** with **Mana Rampage** for glass cannon DPS.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Rerolling Tips">
Always keep a "decent" trait like **Noble** while rolling for Mythics. Don't spend all your spins and end up with "Average" (the common trait with zero buffs).
</Callout>

</Section>
