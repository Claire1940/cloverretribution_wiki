<Section icon={Shield} title="Magic Knight Ranking and Quests">
The Magic Knight ranking system has received an overhaul, with new quests and promotion mechanics designed to provide a clearer progression path. Players can now earn Skill Points (SP) through promotions, which are essential for unlocking nodes in the expanded skill trees.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enroll as a Magic Knight">
    Reach at least Level 100 and talk to the Wizard King in Clover Capital to enroll. You will start as Junior Magic Knight 1.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Complete Orders and Gain XP">
    Take orders from the mission board in Clover Capital. These offer significant XP with a 10-minute cooldown. Normal questing also contributes to XP.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Undertake Promotion Quests">
    Once enough XP is accumulated, the mission board will offer a promotion quest. Complete this to rank up.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Earn Skill Points (SP)">
    Each promotion rewards SP, which are used to unlock nodes in the various skill trees.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Manage Promotion Cooldowns">
    Promotions have a cooldown, starting at 26 minutes for Junior Magic Knight 1 and increasing by 6 minutes per rank.
  </Step>
</Steps>

This structured progression system encourages active participation in the game's world, ensuring players are rewarded for their dedication. The addition of 25 new quests to the mission board provides ample opportunities for advancement, reducing reliance on specific, potentially difficult, tasks.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and Magic Adjustments">
The **Clover Retribution update** includes several reworks and bug fixes impacting combat and magic, most notably for Dark and Wind magic, as well as Devil Union and Spirit Dive mechanics. These adjustments aim to balance power levels and resolve previous exploits.

**Summary of Combat Adjustments:**

| Feature         | Old State                                      | New State                                            | Impact                                             |
|:----------------|:-----------------------------------------------|:-----------------------------------------------------|:---------------------------------------------------|
| Inventory Load  | Slow, noticeable delays                        | Significantly faster loading                         | Improved user experience, less downtime            |
| Broom Flying    | Fast on PC, less effective on mobile/console   | Reworked speed, increased height, 3-sec speed boost | Better for mobile/console, slower for PC veterans  |
| Legion          | Macro-friendly, strong                         | Pieces have health bars, draw aggro, despawn if far | Massive nerf, prevents Kraken macroing             |
| Crits           | Standard mechanics                             | Reworked, making Dark magic extremely powerful       | Dark magic builds now highly effective             |
| Level Cap       | 405                                            | **500**                                              | Requires more grinding for max level prestiges     |
| Spirit Dives    | No mana cost to sustain                        | Now cost mana to sustain, toggleable                 | Requires resource management                       |
| Aetherion's Blessing & Devil Union | Could be used together, bugged interactions            | No buffs from Aetherion's Blessing with Devil equipped | Fixes exploit, balances power                      |
| Devil Union % Cap | Lower                                          | Increased to **45%**                                 | More control over Devil Union, future mana costs   |
| Shadow Magic    | Not available                                  | Added with unique spells, no 4L spell yet            | New build possibilities, currently no stat scaling |
| Wind Magic      | Extremely powerful, 3-second Kraken kills      | Nerfed, Kraken kills now 8-10 seconds                | Balanced power, still strong but not overpowered   |

Shadow Magic, a new addition, offers unique abilities like `Night Coffin`, which can deal thousands of damage. While it currently lacks stat scaling (making 100% CON builds optimal), its potential is significant. The rework of Devil Union and Spirit Dive checks should prevent previous bugs where players could combine incompatible abilities.
