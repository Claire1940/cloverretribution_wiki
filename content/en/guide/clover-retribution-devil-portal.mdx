<Section icon={Swords} title="Securing the Devil Contract">

Once you enter the **clover retribution devil portal**, you are faced with a choice that will permanently alter your character's progression. You can either negotiate a Bound Contract or challenge the Devil to an Unbound Contract. This decision depends entirely on your combat capability and your willingness to sacrifice permanent character stats.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter the Rift">
    Ensure you are Level 150+ and approach the portal. Once inside, you will find a platform where the Devil resides.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose Contract Type">
    Interact with the Devil. You will be prompted to choose between a Bound or Unbound agreement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete the Requirement">
    For Bound, select your sacrifice. For Unbound, prepare for a high-difficulty 1v1 duel.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Finalize the Union">
    Once the conditions are met, the Devil will vanish, and you will gain access to Devil Mode (press T).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Unbound Advantage">
The Unbound Contract is objectively superior because it grants 80% of the Devil's stats without any negative trade-offs. However, the 1v1 fight is grueling. If you fail the fight, you lose the portal opportunity.
</Callout>

If you choose the Bound route, you must offer a sacrifice. These sacrifices are permanent and can only be reversed by removing the Devil entirely at the Devil's Advocate NPC for 50,000 Gold.

| Sacrifice Type | Penalty Description | Recommended For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Soul** | Permanent Soul Drain debuff | Hardcore players only |
| **Health** | Permanent loss of 15% Max HP | High-defense builds |
| **Wealth** | 50% reduction in all Gold earned | Endgame players |
| **Potential** | 50% EXP loss for Mastery/Magic | Max-level characters |

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Devil Personalities and Leveling">

Not all Devils behave the same way. Your Devil will have one of several personalities, which dictates how you must level it up. Leveling your Devil is essential to unlock its full potential and eventually transition into a Chibi Devil.

**Personality Leveling Requirements:**
- **Mysterious**: Requires killing event bosses or completing Level 150+ quests.
- **Vengeful**: Requires defeating players of a similar level or soloing bosses.
- **Arrogant**: Requires soloing bosses (100% damage) while not in a party.

<Callout type="info" title="Devilish Mana">
Using Devil Mode consumes Devilish Mana. You can replenish this resource by consuming Soul Fragments, which drop from various NPCs while you have an active Devil contract.
</Callout>

To further enhance your Devil, you can collect specific Soul items that drop from elite enemies throughout the world. These items not only recover 15% of your Devilish Mana but also provide permanent stat increases.

| Item Name | Stat Boost | Drop Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Soul of Nimbleness** | Dexterity Increase | Wolf / Grimoire Gauntlet |
| **Soul of Wisdom** | Intelligence Increase | Alpha Wolf / Wind Monks |
| **Soul of Fortitude** | Constitution Increase | Desert Claw / Stone Guardian |
| **Soul of Valor** | Strength Increase | Ogre / Yeti / Juggernaut |
