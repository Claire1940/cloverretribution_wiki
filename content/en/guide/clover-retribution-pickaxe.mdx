<Section icon={Target} title="How to Obtain Pickaxe Blueprints">

Acquiring a **clover retribution pickaxe** is a two-step process involving blueprint acquisition and subsequent crafting. Unlike standard weapons that might drop from bosses, pickaxes are strictly profession-based items. You must locate Akumir Master Miner, who serves as the primary vendor for all mining-related schematics.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Aestherstone Lake">
    Travel to Aestherstone Lake to find the Ogol Profession Master. Complete the "Learning Professions" quest to unlock the system.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find Akumir Master Miner">
    Akumir is typically located near mining nodes or within the profession hubs. He sells the essential blueprints for both tools and refined materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Purchase Blueprints">
    Buy the Copper Pickaxe Blueprint and the Iron Pickaxe Blueprint. Each will cost you 1000 Gold.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Gather Materials">
    Mine raw ore (using a basic tool if available or purchasing raw materials) and use Akumir's Ingot blueprints to create the necessary bars.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Craft at the Blacksmith">
    Head to a forge and use your Blacksmithing skill to turn the blueprints and ingots into a functional pickaxe.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Blueprint Requirement">
Note that the Copper Pickaxe Blueprint is specifically purchased from Akumir Master Miner, whereas many other weapon blueprints are purchased from Ogol. Do not confuse the two NPCs.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Blacksmithing and Refining Materials">

Owning a **clover retribution pickaxe** is only half the battle. To maintain your gear and progress to higher tiers, you must master the refining process. Mining provides raw ore, but the Blacksmithing profession is required to turn that ore into useful items. Akumir Master Miner provides the blueprints for ingots, which are the fundamental building blocks for all advanced tools.

| Material | Raw Source | Refined Form | Blueprint Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Copper** | Copper Ore Nodes | Copper Ingot | 1000 Gold |
| **Iron** | Iron Ore Nodes | Iron Ingot | 1000 Gold |
| **Gold** | Rare Gold Veins | Gold Ingot | Varies |

The refining process requires you to have the blueprint in your inventory and stand near a furnace. As you level up your Mining and Blacksmithing, you will unlock the ability to craft more complex items, including the Iron Pickaxe, which significantly reduces the time spent at each ore node.

<Callout type="info" title="Vendor Location">
Remember that Ogol Profession Master in Aestherstone Lake sells the basic weapon blueprints (Broken Sword, Copper Hammer), but Akumir is the only one who sells the pickaxe-specific schematics.
</Callout>
