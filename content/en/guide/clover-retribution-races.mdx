<Section icon={Shield} title="Hybrid Paladin: The Pinnacle Progression Guide">

Becoming a Tier 4 Blessed Hybrid Paladin is considered the ultimate goal for any serious player. This process is separate from standard race rolling and requires intensive farming of high-level bosses.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Roll">
    First, you must roll the base Hybrid Paladin race. Look for visual cues like a halo or wings to determine your starting Tier (1 through 4).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Soul Fragment Farming">
    Hunt the Corrupted Bat Vampire Lord or Tier 3+ Devils. These bosses drop True Paladin Soul Fragments, which are essential for Tier progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Achieving Blessed Status">
    You need exactly 21 Soul Fragments to guarantee "Blessed" status. Each fragment has a 1% chance to trigger it early, but 20 is the pity threshold.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Grace Weaving">
    Craft Graces using catalysts from bosses like the Etherstone Golem. Graces provide permanent buffs such as 25% stronger shields or 10% reduced mana consumption.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Fragment Drop Rates">
The drop rate for True Paladin Soul Fragments is estimated at approximately 1.8%. Expect to grind for 10-15 hours to fully max out your Tier progression.
</Callout>
