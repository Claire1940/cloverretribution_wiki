<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Strategy and Boss Mechanics">

Fighting the **clover retribution vampire lord** requires high mobility or a dedicated tank. Lucius utilizes high-damage AOE attacks and a shield mechanic that makes him formidable for solo players under level 200.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Normal Lucius">
    - **Shield**: Regenerates shield until 50% HP.
    - **AOE**: Telegraphs white circles on the ground.
    - **Speed**: Moderate movement, easy to kite.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Corrupted Lucius">
    - **No Shield**: Shield breaks permanently after 50% HP.
    - **Purple Aura**: Massive damage boost to all skills.
    - **Rare Drop**: Only variant that drops the **Vampire Spear**.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

**Boss Attack Patterns:**
1. **Lunge Attack**: A quick dash toward the player with high physical damage.
2. **Blood AOE**: Large circles appear on the floor; stay out of these or take massive tick damage.
3. **Shield Burst**: Lucius emits a shockwave when his shield is hit, pushing back melee attackers.

| Attack Type | Damage Range | Avoidance Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ground Slam** | High (Physical) | Dash backward when he raises his arm. |
| **Blood Spikes** | Moderate (Magic) | Move laterally to avoid the tracking spikes. |
| **Lucius Ultimate** | Fatal | Hide behind pillars or use wall-jumping cheese. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Cheese Strategy">
If you are struggling, use a Divine Restriction (DR) tank to draw aggro and jump onto the castle wall edges. Lucius often bugs out on the geometry, allowing mages to safely pelt him with spells from a distance.
</Callout>

<Section icon={FlaskConical} title="Unlocking the Elder Vampire Race">

The Elder Vampire is arguably the best race for mages in Clover Retribution. While you can spin for it (2% chance), the most reliable method is through the "Infection" questline provided by the NPC Dorian.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Scarlet Lily Quest">
    Find Dorian inside the Vampire Castle. He will task you with finding 5 **Scarlet Lilies**. These spawn randomly in place of regular Mana Flowers or Fire Lilies in the overworld.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Daily Grind">
    Complete Dorian's daily quests for 15 days. You will need to farm Vampire Soldiers, Elites, and eventually Lucius himself to gather materials like the **Phantom Flask** and **Cursed Sigil**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Infection">
    Find an Awakened Elder Vampire player and have them bite you. You have a 5% chance to receive the **Scarlet Affliction** trait. If you die during the bite, the infection failed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Consumption">
    While you have the Scarlet Affliction trait, consume **Pure Vampire Blood** (dropped by the Vampire Lord). This will reset your race into one of the three vampire tiers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Infection Rates">
When consuming the blood under Scarlet Affliction, the rates are: **Fletchling (70%)**, **Vampire (29%)**, and **Elder Vampire (1%)**. If you get Fletchling, it is often better to reset and try the infection again rather than farming the massive amount of blood needed to evolve it.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Activity} title="Race Stats and Evolution Requirements">

Once you have become a vampire, you can upgrade your tier using Pure Vampire Blood or by crafting the **Bloodbound Relic** at the Pot Maker NPC (requires Alchemy Level 70).

| Race Tier | Key Stats | Evolution Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fletchling** | +5 Int, +5 Con, 1% HP Regen | 10 Pure Blood to Vampire |
| **Vampire** | +10 Int, +10 Con, 1.5% HP Regen | 20 Pure Blood to Elder |
| **Elder Vampire** | +20 Int, +20 Con, 2.5% HP Regen | 20 Pure Blood to Awakened |

**Elder Vampire Passive Benefits:**
- **Immunity**: Complete immunity to Poison.
- **Mana Pool**: +15% Max Mana and +3 Flat Mana Regen.
- **Damage**: +10% Magic Damage.
- **Heightened Senses**: Ability to see which players have Devils equipped.
