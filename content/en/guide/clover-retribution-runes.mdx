<Section icon={Target} title="Pillar Locations and Activation Steps">

Once you have all four **clover retribution runes**, you must navigate the Grasslands to find their respective stone pillars. These pillars are identifiable by their large size and inverted triangle engravings. Activating them requires you to interact with the stone and "Insert" the specific rune.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Shattered Earth Pillar">
    Head toward the massive skull head near the Alpha Wolf spawn. On a hill to the left, guarded by goblins, you will find the first pillar. Insert the Rune of Shattered Earth here.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Raging Tempest Pillar">
    Fly or run toward the Wind Monks area, which is past the Bandit Camp. The pillar is located in a clearing surrounded by trees. Insert the Rune of Raging Tempest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Endless Challenge Pillar">
    Navigate toward the Kingdom castle. Between the castle stairs and the Ogre area, tucked into a corner, stands the third pillar. Insert the Rune of Endless Challenge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Colossal Wrath Pillar">
    The final pillar is located right at the center of the lake south of Unnamed Village, directly adjacent to the portal gateway. Insert the Rune of Colossal Wrath to ignite the portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
If a raid fails, the portal will enter a **15-minute cooldown**. During this time, the pillars cannot be reactivated, so it is vital to coordinate with your team before entering.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Aetherstone Golem Raid Mechanics">

The Aetherstone Golem is a formidable opponent that utilizes bludgeoning damage. He is completely immune to poison, so players relying on poison-based grimoires or weapons will need to adjust their builds. The fight is divided into distinct phases based on the Golem's health pool.

| Attack Name | Description | How to Counter |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stone Fist** | Summons a fist from below the player. | Perfect Dodge or Parry when the ground glows. |
| **Ground Slam** | A heavy slam dealing massive AoE damage. | Jump or stay out of the immediate impact zone. |
| **Spinning AoE** | The Golem spins, hitting all nearby players. | Block or move to a safe distance. |
| **Purple Crystals** | Spawns 6 crystals at 75% health. | **DPS Check**: Destroy all 6 within 30 seconds. |

The **Purple Crystal Phase** is the most common point of failure for solo players. If all six crystals are not destroyed within the 30-second window, every player in the raid area is instantly killed. This mechanic necessitates high burst damage or a coordinated team.

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Tip">
All of the Golem's standard attacks can be **Parried or Perfect Dodged**. Mastering the timing of the Stone Fist is the key to maintaining your guard meter and staying in the fight.
</Callout>

</Section>
