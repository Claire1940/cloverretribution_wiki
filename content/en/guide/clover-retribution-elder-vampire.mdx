<Section icon={Target} title="Acquiring the Elder Vampire Race">
Obtaining the **Elder Vampire** race in Clover Retribution is a multi-step process that combines luck with dedicated farming. While it's possible to spin for the race, the low 2% chance makes it an unreliable method. The more common and reliable path involves a lengthy questline and a unique infection mechanic.

### Method 1: Race Spin (Low Chance)

| Spin Chance | Priority Impact | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| **2% Base Chance** | Slightly increased with max priority. | Not recommended as a primary method due to extreme rarity. |

### Method 2: Infection and Pure Vampire Blood (Primary Method)

This method involves being infected by an **Awakened Elder Vampire** and then using a rare item called **Pure Vampire Blood**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Find an Awakened Elder Vampire">
    Locate a player who possesses the **Awakened Elder Vampire** race and is willing to infect you. This often requires seeking out players in the official Discord server for Clover Retribution.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Get Infected (The Bite)">
    Allow the Awakened Elder Vampire to bite you. There's a **5% chance** for the infection to succeed. If you die, the infection failed. If you don't die and see a red text message, you've acquired the **Scarlet Affliction** trait for 24 hours. You can only attempt infection every 12 hours (or 6 hours with Golden Clover Crop).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Obtain Pure Vampire Blood">
    This is a mythical item that drops from the **Vampire Lord** boss. It's a rare drop, making farming the Vampire Lord a crucial part of the process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Consume Pure Vampire Blood">
    Once you have the **Scarlet Affliction** trait and **Pure Vampire Blood**, consume the blood. This will reset your current race.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Race Transformation">
    After consuming the blood, your race will transform based on these probabilities:
    - **Fletchling (Subpar Vampire)**: ~70% chance
    - **Vampire (Regular Legendary)**: ~29% chance
    - **Elder Vampire (Mythical)**: ~1% chance
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Pure Vampire Blood Acquisition">
Pure Vampire Blood is a mythical item with a very low drop rate from the **Vampire Lord**. This necessitates repeated farming of the boss, which can be a time-consuming endeavor. Consider farming with a party to increase efficiency.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="The Vampire Lord Questline and Farming">
To consistently acquire **Pure Vampire Blood** and progress towards the Elder Vampire race, you'll need to engage in a dedicated questline and farming routine centered around the Vampire Lord. This process also provides valuable crafting materials and XP.

### Locating Dorian and the Vampire Castle

1.  **Start at Unnamed Village:** Head right from the starting point, towards the Medusa raid area.
2.  **Enter the Red Portal:** This portal leads to the vampire/werewolf areas.
3.  **Navigate to Vampire Area:** Follow the path past the wooden wall, and turn left at the split (right is werewolf area).
4.  **Reach the Castle:** Cross the bridge to reach the Vampire Castle.
5.  **Talk to Dorian:** Inside the castle, find NPC Dorian, a Senior Knight, to begin the questline.

### Questline Progression

Dorian offers a series of daily quests that reset every 12 hours (or 6 hours with Golden Clover Crop). Completing these quests is vital for obtaining crafting blueprints and advancing your alchemy level, which is necessary for crafting **Bloodbound Relics**.

| Quest Phase | Objective | Reward/Outcome | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1: Scarlet Lily** | Find 5 Scarlet Lilies | Progresses questline, provides XP. | Daily |
| **Phase 2: Corrupted Vampire Lord** | Kill a Corrupted Vampire Lord | Unlocks further quests, chance for **Pure Vampire Blood** drop. | Daily |
| **Phase 3: Vampire Soldiers** | Defeat 15 Vampire Soldiers | Phantom Flask, significant XP. | Repeat 15 times over days. |
| **Phase 4: Vampire Elites** | Defeat 9 Vampire Elites | Essence Drop (need 10). | Repeat 5 times over days. |
| **Phase 5: Vampire Lords** | Defeat 3 Vampire Lords | Cursed Sigil (need 5), **Bloodbound Relic Blueprint**. | Repeat 5 times over days. |

<Callout type="success" title="Efficient Farming">
To speed up the process, gather a party for farming Vampire Soldiers, Elites, and especially the Vampire Lord. Group efforts make these encounters much faster and more manageable.
</Callout>

### Spawning and Defeating the Corrupted Vampire Lord

1.  **Kill Vampire Soldiers:** Defeat 10-13 Vampire Soldiers around the castle. They drop crafting items like Nocturnal Thread and Crimson Essence, and occasionally Pure Blood Vials (which are generally not useful for Elder Vampire progression).
2.  **Spawn Vampire Elites:** After killing enough soldiers, a message appears: "Stronger vampires can now be seen around the castle." Defeat the three Vampire Elites.
3.  **Spawn Vampire Lucius:** Another message will appear: "A shadow deepens across the land as vampire Lucius awakens, bringing an ancient darkness with him." This indicates the normal Vampire Lord has spawned.
4.  **Corrupted Vampire Lord:** To spawn the **Corrupted Vampire Lord**, you need to defeat three normal Vampire Lords. Each untransformed Vampire Lord kill increases the chance of the next one being corrupted by 30%. A corrupted spawn is indicated by a double message (Vampire Lord and Primal Werewolf spawning) and a purplish aura on the boss.
5.  **Defeat Corrupted Vampire Lord:** The corrupted version is harder but drops **Pure Vampire Blood** more reliably. Utilize terrain (like running up walls) to cheese him with a party, especially if you have a tank or Divine Restriction user to draw aggro.

<Callout type="tip">
The **Bloodbound Relic Blueprint** is a crucial drop from the first time you complete the "Defeat 3 Vampire Lords" quest. This relic is essential for upgrading your vampire race without relying solely on rare blood drops.
</Callout>

After you've successfully transformed into a Fletchling or regular Vampire, you'll want to upgrade to the full **Elder Vampire** and eventually the **Awakened Elder Vampire**. This requires either more **Pure Vampire Blood** or crafting **Bloodbound Relics**.

  <Card title="Bloodbound Relic Method">
    - Craft **Bloodbound Relics** using the blueprint acquired from the questline.

    - A more consistent method for upgrading if you have the crafting materials.
  </Card>

### Crafting Bloodbound Relics

1.  **Acquire Blueprint:** Complete the "Defeat 3 Vampire Lords" quest (the third daily quest phase).
2.  **Reach Alchemy Level 70:** Craft various items to level up your Alchemy skill.
3.  **Gather Materials:** Collect the Phantom Flask (from 15 Vampire Soldiers quest), Essence Drop (from 9 Vampire Elites quest), and Cursed Sigil (from 3 Vampire Lords quest).
4.  **Craft at Pot Maker:** Interact with the Pot Maker NPC and select "Craft your own" to create the **Bloodbound Relic**.

<Callout type="warning" title="Relic Crafting Limit">
You can only craft the **Bloodbound Relic** twice. This means if you get a Fletchling and choose to upgrade it, you might not have enough relics to reach the Awakened Elder Vampire stage without additional Pure Vampire Blood. It's often statistically better to aim for at least a regular Vampire from the initial infection.
</Callout>

### Upgrade Requirements

| Current Race | Target Race | Required Items | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Fletchling** | Vampire | 10 Pure Vampire Blood OR 1 Bloodbound Relic | If starting as Fletchling, consider re-infecting rather than upgrading due to relic limits. |
| **Vampire** | Elder Vampire | 20 Pure Vampire Blood OR 1 Bloodbound Relic | This is the most efficient path if you get a regular Vampire from infection. |
| **Elder Vampire** | Awakened Elder Vampire | 20 Pure Vampire Blood OR 1 Bloodbound Relic | Grants the ability to transform and infect other players. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Optimizing Your Path">
If your initial infection results in a **Fletchling**, it's generally more efficient to reset your race (if you have a storage for your previous race) and attempt the infection process again to aim for a regular **Vampire**. This preserves your limited **Bloodbound Relics** for the final upgrade to **Elder Vampire** and **Awakened Elder Vampire**.
</Callout>
