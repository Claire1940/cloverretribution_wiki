<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Solo Strategy">

Soloing the **clover retribution ogre** requires a disciplined approach. You cannot "tank" its hits unless you have a dedicated defensive build. Instead, you must treat the fight like a dance, reacting to its heavy telegraphed moves while chipping away at its defenses. Follow these steps to ensure a clean kill without burning through your potion inventory.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Engagement">
    Approach the Ogre from the front to bait the **Rock Throw**. Keep your block held as you close the distance. Do not use your high-damage skills yet; save them for the shield-break phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Shield Grind">
    Stick close to the Ogre's legs. When it raises its arms for the **M-Slam**, dash to the right. Immediately follow up with a series of M1 (Light) attacks. Your goal here is purely to deplete the yellow shield bar.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Shield Break">
    Once the shield bar empties, the Ogre will play a falling animation. This is your window. Unleash your most powerful Grimoire spells or weapon skills now. Focus on the head if possible for potential crit modifiers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reset and Repeat">
    After 8 seconds, the Ogre will stand back up and roar, pushing you back. This roar deals no damage but resets its shield. Back off, regenerate your stamina, and prepare to repeat the cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Stamina Management">
Always keep at least 20% of your stamina bar full. If the Ogre starts a Ground Pound and you are out of stamina to dash, you will likely take a lethal hit.
</Callout>

</Section>
