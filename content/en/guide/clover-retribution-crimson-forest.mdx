<Section icon={Target} title="Summoning the Crimson Witch">
Summoning the Crimson Witch is a multi-step process that involves collecting four distinct items from various bosses across the map. Once collected, these items must be inserted into specific floating blood orbs to activate the summoning ritual.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Summoning Items">
    You'll need to defeat specific world bosses to acquire the four summoning items. Each item has a unique drop rate, so prepare for some grinding.
    <Callout type="tip">
      Focus on farming these bosses with a group to increase your efficiency and chances of getting the drops quickly.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Blood Orbs">
    There are four floating blood orbs strategically placed around the map. You'll need to know the exact location of each orb and which item corresponds to it. The portal to the Crimson Forest is located just south of Alpha Wolf.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Insert Items into Orbs">
    Once all four items are collected, proceed to their respective blood orbs and insert them. A message will appear confirming the witch has been summoned.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Enter the Crimson Forest">
    Head through the red portal that appears after summoning. The Crimson Witch will be waiting in the center of the Crimson Forest.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Crimson Witch Abilities and Tactics">
The Crimson Witch is a formidable opponent, dealing both magic and bleed damage. Understanding her attack patterns and knowing how to counter them is crucial for survival. She starts with a 15,000 HP shield that only needs to be broken once.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Blood Explosion">
    - **Effect**: Summons a blood explosion on every player in the Crimson Forest.
    - **Defense**: Can be dodged or parried. Timely reactions are key.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Blood Hydras">
    - **Effect**: Spawns hydras that spit homing blood orbs.
    - **Defense**: Can be dodged. While less damaging, ignoring them entirely can lead to accumulated damage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Crucifixion">
    - **Effect**: Crucifies anyone within its radius.
    - **Defense**: Cannot be parried. Dodge timing is very late (about 2 seconds after the red circle fills).
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Phase">
Once the Crimson Witch's health drops below 75%, the portal to the Crimson Forest will close. This means if you die or run out of mana, you cannot re-enter without a Spatial magic user to teleport you back.
</Callout>

**Essential Party Composition:**
Having a **Spatial magic user** in your party is almost a necessity. They can teleport players back into the raid area, ensuring continuous engagement even if someone falls. Alternatively, having an alt account with Spatial magic waiting at spawn can serve the same purpose. Even if you're powerful enough to solo her, it's inefficient; a group yields significantly more drops.

</Section>
