<Section icon={Target} title="The Mana Collection Totem and Its Use">
The Mana Collection Totem is an essential tool for this quest. It's not a consumable item, meaning you can use it multiple times without fear of it breaking or running out. Understanding how to deploy and utilize it effectively is crucial for gathering corrupted mana.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire the Totem">
    The Mana Collection Totem is given to you by the **Owl NPC** in the Tundra as part of the main questline. Ensure you have progressed far enough to receive this item.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate a Corrupted Yeti">
    Travel to the Tundra and seek out a corrupted Yeti. They are often found in specific areas within the region.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Place the Totem">
    Once near a corrupted Yeti, place the totem on the ground. It will project a visible radius around it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Keep the Yeti in Range">
    The most critical step is to keep the corrupted Yeti within the totem's collection radius. You may need to "strafe" or kite the Yeti around the totem to ensure continuous mana collection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Monitor Mana Collection">
    A notification or UI element will indicate the progress of mana collection, typically showing around 33-34 units collected per successful placement. Repeat this process approximately three times to complete the mana requirement.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Totem Efficiency">
The Mana Collection Totem recharges and can be used multiple times. You only need to place it down and keep the Yeti within its radius until a segment of mana is collected, then you can repeat the process with the same totem.
</Callout>
