<Section icon={Map} title="Accessing Artemis Tower in Clover Retribution">
Artemis Tower, also known as the Corin Tower, is a significant landmark and quest hub in Clover Retribution. Previously, reaching this location involved a tedious climb, but recent updates have introduced a convenient fast travel option, streamlining your journey. This section will guide you on how to efficiently access Artemis Tower and begin your quest.

To access Artemis Tower, you first need to progress through the main questline until you are directed to the Capital. Once in the Capital, after dealing with the Shadow Hand goons, you will be instructed to head to Artemis Tower.

**Video Highlights:**

After defeating the Wind Mages, you will be tasked with retrieving an item from the Aetherstone Golem. This boss fight requires careful preparation and execution. Following this, you'll be directed to speak with Fabio in the Devil's Route. Accessing this area requires a specific event and password.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Defeat Wind Mages">
    Locate and defeat 15 Wind Mages found outside Artemis Tower. These enemies can be challenging in groups, so prioritize crowd control or focused damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Confront Aetherstone Golem">
    Travel to the portal near the starter town to find and defeat the Aetherstone Golem. This boss drops a crucial item needed for your quest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Access Devil's Route">
    Head to the desert village and locate the NPC involved in the "someone knows something" event. Talk to them and use the password "darkness unveils the truth" to open the Devil's Route.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Meet Fabio">
    Inside the Devil's Route, find and converse with Fabio. He will provide vital information about corrupted mana and direct you to the Capital's advisor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Post-Artemis Tower Progression">
Completing the Artemis Tower questline is a significant milestone, opening up new areas and advancing the overarching narrative. The information gathered here sets the stage for investigations into corrupted mana and further challenges across the world of Clover Retribution.

Your journey will lead you to the Tundra to find an 'owl' (an NPC, not an actual owl) and then to collect corrupted mana from yetis and later from stone guardians. This involves placing a mana collection totem and keeping the corrupted enemies within its range.
