<Section icon={Target} title="Optimal Early Game Leveling (Levels 0-90)">
The early stages of Clover Retribution are crucial for building a strong foundation. Efficiently clearing initial quests and understanding early-game mechanics will accelerate your progress.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Quests in Grasslands Forest (Levels 0-20)">
    Start by heading to the **Grasslands Forest**. Locate the quest giver near a bridge to accept the "Help the mysterious stranger" quest. Defeating the Alpha Wolf will not only grant experience but also unlock the Broom for faster travel.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clover Capital Ogre & Wolf Hunters (Levels 20-60)">
    Proceed to **Clover Capital**. Between levels 20-40, seek out the ogre with a purple aura for substantial experience. From levels 40-60, find the quest giver near the stairs within the city who will task you with defeating six Wolf Hunters found outside the city walls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Riddlebone Realm Exploration (Levels 60-90)">
    At level 60, enter the **Riddlebone Realm** via the glowing orange portal in the starting area. Here, you can defeat pumpkins for XP. From levels 70-80, investigate a web-covered tree for a quest to defeat corrupted skeletons. Finally, between levels 80-90, descend into the church with red windows to defeat a Riddlebone (requires 25 Strength).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
Do not neglect your main quests! They often provide the most significant experience rewards and unlock critical game features and areas.
</Callout>
