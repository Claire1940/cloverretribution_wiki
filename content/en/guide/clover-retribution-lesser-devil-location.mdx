Lesser Devils are formidable mini-bosses in Clover Retribution, essential for acquiring valuable resources and progressing certain game mechanics. These creatures, often referred to as Juggernauts, are not static and appear across multiple spawn points on the map. Lesser Devils have a base HP of approximately 5,400 and are protected by a shield with about 1,900 HP. <Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Tips for Lesser Devils">
While Lesser Devils have predictable attack patterns, mastering combat against them can significantly improve your farming efficiency and reduce damage taken.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Attack Patterns">
    - **Slam Attack**: A straightforward, powerful hit.
    - **Kick Attack**: Another direct attack.
    - **Poison Effect**: Both attacks inflict a poison debuff, dealing minor continuous damage.
    - **Double Attack Cycle**: Every few attack cycles, they might throw two attacks before your perfect dodge cooldown resets.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Defensive Strategies">
    - **Perfect Dodge**: Time your dodges to avoid all damage and the poison effect.
    - **Parry**: Parrying can block the main attack damage but still results in poison.
    - **Alternating Defense**: If you can't perfect dodge every attack (e.g., without Divine Restriction), alternate between perfect dodging and parrying.
    - **Stand Your Ground**: For less experienced players, staying in one spot and focusing on defense is often safer.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="tip" title="Divine Restriction Build">
If you're running a Divine Restriction build, you can perfect dodge every attack, making Lesser Devils much easier to handle. For other builds, be mindful of the poison and your dodge cooldowns.
</Callout>
