### Core Movement and Interaction

The fundamental controls in Clover Retribution are intuitive, allowing players to navigate the world and interact with elements seamlessly. Understanding these basic inputs is crucial for exploration, questing, and avoiding danger.

| Action        | PC Keybind     | Mobile Touchscreen | Description                                   |
|---------------|----------------|--------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| **Move**      | WASD           | On-screen joystick | Controls character movement (forward, back, left, right). |
| **Attack/Interact** | LMB (Left Mouse Button) | Tap/Left side of screen | Primary attack or interact with objects and NPCs. |
| **Heavy Attack** | RMB (Right Mouse Button) | Hold Tap/Right side of screen | Executes a stronger, often slower, attack. |
| **Jump**      | Space          | On-screen button   | Allows your character to jump over obstacles or reach higher ground. |
| **Run/Sprint** | Shift / Alt    | Tap to toggle      | Increases movement speed for faster travel. |
