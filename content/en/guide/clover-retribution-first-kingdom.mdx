<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and Leveling Strategies in the First Kingdom">
Efficient leveling is key to progressing quickly through the **Clover Retribution First Kingdom**. After defeating Bandits, you'll move on to more challenging enemies like the Mysterious Traveler's foes and eventually the Alpha Wolf. <Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Hybrid Builds Early On">
While it might seem appealing to split your stats, dedicating to a single build type (e.g., full Dexterity for melee or full Intelligence for magic) is far more effective in the early game. Hybrid builds often lack the focused damage or survivability needed for efficient grinding.
</Callout>

Once you reach around level 75, you'll be ready to transition towards the Clover Kingdom areas. This involves defeating Season Mercenaries, which offer substantial XP. Grouping with other players can make these encounters much easier and faster.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Travel to Tundra">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find Yeti Horn NPC">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Frozen Lilies">

</Steps>
