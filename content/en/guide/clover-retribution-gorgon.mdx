<Section icon={Swords} title="Conquering the Medusa Raid for Gorgon Rewards">
The Medusa raid is a critical activity for any player looking to acquire **Gorgon-related items** in Clover Retribution. This raid boss is a formidable opponent, but with the right strategy, you can efficiently farm its valuable drops.

### Medusa Raid Requirements and Mechanics
To challenge Medusa, you must reach **level 225**. However, only the party leader needs to meet this requirement; other party members can join regardless of their level. The raid offers three difficulties: Easy, Medium, and Hard.

| Difficulty | HP (Approx.) | Drop Rate Increase |
|------------|--------------|--------------------|
| Easy       | 40,000       | None               |
| Medium     | 100,000      | None               |
| Hard       | 180,000      | None               |

<Callout type="warning" title="Difficulty vs. Rewards">
Medusa's damage output does not significantly change across difficulties, and more importantly, **drop rates are not increased on harder difficulties**. Therefore, it is highly recommended to stick to **Easy mode** to save time and resources.
</Callout>

Medusa has four primary attacks:
1.  **Threat-based Arrow**: Targets the player with the highest threat, can pass through walls. Dodge or use Divine Restriction (DR) builds to avoid explosion damage.
2.  **AoE Scream**: Deals 400-500 damage. Dodge about half a second after the red circle fills.
3.  **Rain of Arrows**: Arrows fall in a small area. Move out of the circle or tank the damage while continuing to attack.
4.  **Petrifying Gaze**: Medusa glows purple and screams. If your character is facing her, you will be petrified for about 7 seconds. **Look away** to avoid this stun.

Medusa will dive underground and change locations approximately every 40 seconds, moving between eight random spots.

<Section icon={Target} title="Medusa Raid Strategy and Efficient Farming">
Efficiently clearing the Medusa raid requires understanding her movement patterns and exploiting certain mechanics. While she moves frequently, a "cheese" method can significantly speed up your runs.

### Optimal Builds and Cheese Method
For mage players, Dark, Blood, or a good Fourleaf Flame/Wind build are effective. Otherwise, a **Divine Restriction (DR) build** offers the easiest time due to extended dodge invulnerability frames.

**The Medusa Reset Cheese:**
This method allows you to prevent Medusa from moving around the map, saving valuable time.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate the Raid">
    Start the Medusa raid on Easy difficulty.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Observe Medusa's Dive">
    When Medusa dives underground to change locations (approximately every 40 seconds), pay close attention.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger Reset (Solo)">
    If playing solo, **reset your character** as soon as she dives. This will cause her to reappear in the middle of the arena.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Trigger Reset (Party)">
    In a party, have **all members reset their characters** simultaneously as she dives. She will then reappear in the middle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Repeat and Farm">
    Continue this process. This completely skips the chase phase, allowing for much faster raid clears. You are limited by your five lives per raid.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Important Note">
This cheese method may be patched in future updates. It is advisable to utilize it while it is still functional to gather desired **Gorgon tokens** and materials.
</Callout>

</Section>
