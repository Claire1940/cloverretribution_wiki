  <Step num="2" title="Gather Required Gold">

<Section icon={Target} title="Advanced Flight Techniques and Mana Management">

Mastering the **clover retribution broom** involves more than just holding the forward key. Advanced players use flight to gain tactical advantages in PVP and to dodge massive AOE attacks from world bosses. Since flight is tied directly to your mana pool, your build significantly impacts your mobility.

**Mana Management Strategies:**
- **Intelligence Scaling**: Increasing your INT stat not only boosts spell damage but also expands your mana pool, allowing for longer flight durations.
- **Pulsed Flying**: Instead of holding Shift constantly, tap it in short bursts to maintain momentum while reducing the drain on your energy bar.
- **Altitude Conservation**: Climbing costs more mana than horizontal movement. Reach your desired height early, then glide at a steady pace.

| Feature | Impact on Flight | Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mana Wisp** | Passive Regen | Always have active during long trips. |
| **High Wind** | Pushback | Fly lower to the ground to avoid wind resistance. |
| **Weight** | Speed Penalty | Heavy armor can slightly reduce flight acceleration. |

<Callout type="info" title="Combat Flight">
In the current 2026 patch, being hit while on your broom has a chance to dismount you. If you are a mage, use your long-range spells to clear a path before flying through aggressive mob zones.
</Callout>
