<Section icon={Target} title="How to Obtain the Wolf Iron Jacket">
There are two primary methods to acquire the **Wolf Iron Jacket** in *Clover Retribution*: by defeating **Wolf Hunters** or through crafting. Both methods require some dedication but are achievable for most players.

### Method 1: Defeating Wolf Hunters

Wolf Hunters are specific NPCs that can drop the **Wolf Iron Jacket** upon defeat. These enemies are typically found in areas where wolves are prevalent. Farming these enemies is a direct way to acquire the jacket, though the drop rate might vary.

<Callout type="warning">
Be prepared for a grind when farming Wolf Hunters, as drop rates can be low. Optimizing your combat build and bringing health potions can improve efficiency.
</Callout>

### Method 2: Crafting the Wolf Iron Jacket

For those who prefer a more controlled approach, the Wolf Iron Jacket can be crafted. This method requires specific resources and the assistance of a Wolf-Iron Craftsman.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate a Wolf-Iron Craftsman">
    Find a **Wolf-Iron Craftsman** NPC in the game world. These craftsmen specialize in creating gear from wolf-related materials. Their location might vary, so exploring different villages or outposts is recommended.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Gold">
    You will need **1,625 Gold** to pay for the crafting service. Gold can be acquired through various in-game activities such as completing quests, defeating enemies, or selling unwanted items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Crafting">
    Interact with the Wolf-Iron Craftsman and select the option to craft the Wolf Iron Jacket. Confirm the crafting process, understanding that there is a **10% crafting rate**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Crafting offers a guaranteed attempt, but success is not assured. Stocking up on gold for multiple attempts might be necessary if luck isn't on your side.
</Callout>
