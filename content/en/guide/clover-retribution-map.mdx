<Section icon={Swords} title="Mid-Game Hub: Clover Kingdom and Shadow Hands">

Once you reach level 75, the **clover retribution map** opens up significantly as you move toward the Clover Capital. This area serves as a central hub for mid-game players and introduces more complex enemy types, such as the Shadow Hands and Ogres. The Capital is also where you will find high-tier vendors and specialized quest NPCs.

The Shadow Hands are a particularly important mob type because they are "squishy" (low health) but deal high damage. Learning their attack patterns is vital for survival. During your time here, you may also encounter World Events, indicated by "Five Star" notifications in the chat, which signal the spawn of elite challenges.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Late-Game Hazards: Tundra and Desert Regions">

The Tundra and Desert represent the most challenging environments on the **clover retribution map**. These zones feature environmental hazards that deal tick damage to unprepared players. In the Tundra, you must deal with the "Frost" effect, while the Desert features high-HP enemies like Scorpions and the Apez.

To survive the Tundra, players must craft the **Frostweave Regalia**. This armor set requires materials gathered from the Yeti mobs and Frozen Lilies found along the riverbanks. The Tundra Village also houses the Master Blacksmith, who is responsible for upgrading your weapons using Mana Crystals.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Yeti Horns">
    Defeat Yetis in the Tundra entrance to gather horns. Watch out for their "Roar" stun.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Frozen Lilies">
    Follow the river leading into the Tundra to find these blue plants growing near the ice.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Craft Frostweave Regalia">
    Visit the NPC in the Tundra Village. The recipe costs 3,250 gold and prevents frost damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade at Blacksmith">
    Once protected, use the Village Blacksmith to enhance your weapon's scaling and damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Zone | Hazard Type | Required Gear | Primary Mobs |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tundra** | Frost Damage | Frostweave Regalia | Yetis, Ice Golems |
| **Desert** | High HP Mobs | High DPS Weapons | Scorpions, Apez |
| **Tundra River** | Navigation | Broom/Mount | Frozen Lily Nodes |

<Callout type="success" title="Corrupted Mobs">
Look for mobs with a purple aura in the Tundra. These are "Corrupted" versions that have significantly more health and damage but offer vastly superior drop rates and XP bonuses.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Navigation} title="Navigation Tips and Fast Travel">

Traveling across the massive **clover retribution map** can be time-consuming. To optimize your journey, you should prioritize unlocking the Broom mount early and utilizing the teleportation network.

Dory is the primary NPC for fast travel. He can be found in most major villages and will teleport you to other discovered hubs for a gold fee. The price varies based on the distance between the two locations.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock the Broom">
    Complete the Alpha Wolf quest at level 40. This is your primary mode of transport.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find Dory NPCs">
    Interact with Dory in the Desert, Tundra, and Clover Capital to register the waypoints.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Gold">
    Teleportation costs between 500 to 1,500 gold. Keep a reserve for quick travel during timed events.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Broom Upgrades">
You can upgrade your broom using Starred Spheres. A leveled-up broom has significantly higher flight speed and better maneuverability, making it much easier to dodge Yeti attacks while flying over the Tundra.
</Callout>
