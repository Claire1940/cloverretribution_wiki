<Section icon={Swords} title="Kraken Battle Strategy: Defeating the Beast">
Engaging the Kraken requires a strategic approach, as it possesses unique abilities and considerable health. Understanding its attack patterns and weaknesses is crucial for survival and victory.

The Kraken's primary threat is its ability to slow targets, followed by powerful area-of-effect (AoE) and spin attacks. It also has a devastating underground phase where it can one-shot lower-level players.

  <Card title="Kraken Abilities">

    - **Underground Phase**: The Kraken dives underground, then resurfaces, often targeting and one-shotting weaker players.
    - **High Damage**: Even without specific mechanics, its basic attacks deal substantial damage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Recommended Tactics">

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Utilizing Kraken Tokens: The Artemis Tower Shop">
Once you've accumulated enough Kraken Tokens, you can exchange them for powerful items, including the crucial Cestus weapon, which is essential for unlocking and mastering the Cestus class.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Kraken Tokens">
    Defeat the Kraken multiple times until you have at least five Kraken Tokens. Remember, the daily quest guarantees one token on the first clear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Teleport to Artemis Tower">
    Use a teleporter to quickly travel to Artemis Tower. This is a central hub where many important NPCs and shops are located.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Cat NPC">
    At Artemis Tower, find the specific Cat NPC (often described as having a "stick" or "broom"). This NPC is your vendor for Kraken-related items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Purchase Cestus Weapon">
    Interact with the Cat NPC and browse their inventory. You can purchase the Cestus weapon here, which is necessary to begin your Cestus mastery journey. Other items like the Lantern or Staff might also be available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
The Cestus weapon requires 35 mastery to unlock the Cestus class. After obtaining the weapon and reaching the required mastery, you'll likely need to complete a follow-up quest (often just killing the Kraken again) to fully acquire the class.
</Callout>
