<Section icon={Target} title="The Process of Summoning a Dragon">
Summoning a dragon at the Wishing Well is a straightforward process once you have successfully gathered all seven unique Dragonball Artifacts of a specific type. Each artifact is numbered from 1 to 7, and you must possess one of each number for the summon to be successful.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect 7 Unique Artifacts">
    Participate in Dragon Ball events to find and collect all seven Dragonball Artifacts (numbers 1-7) of the same type (e.g., all Corrupted).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Travel to the Wishing Well">
    Head to the Long Forgotten Tower where the Wishing Well is located. It is often found next to the NPC Timeon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Interact with the Well">
    Approach the Wishing Well and interact with it. You will be prompted to insert your collected artifacts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Choose Your Wish">
    Once the dragon appears, it will present you with a list of wishes corresponding to its reward table. Select the wish that best suits your needs. For the coveted **Clover Retribution Wishing Well Sword** type rewards like the 4-Leaf Grimoire evolution, you'll need to have summoned Maligon.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Note">
Having two artifacts of the same number will prevent you from summoning a dragon. Ensure your collection consists of seven distinct numbered artifacts before attempting a summon.
</Callout>
