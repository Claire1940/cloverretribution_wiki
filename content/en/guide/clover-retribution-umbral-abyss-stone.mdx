<Section icon={Swords} title="Boss Mechanics and Attack Patterns">

Surviving the hunt for the **clover retribution umbral abyss stone** requires recognizing Medusa's telegraphs. Her kit is designed to punish immobile players and those who fail to manage their "Threat" levels. Threat is tracked on the right side of your damage percentage; the player with the highest threat will be the primary target for her most lethal projectiles.

| Attack Name | Description | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Homing Arrow** | Fires a projectile that penetrates walls. | Jump and dodge to avoid the explosion AOE. |
| **AOE Scream** | A red circle fills, dealing 400-500 damage. | Press Q (dodge) 0.5 seconds after the circle fills. |
| **Arrow Rain** | Projectiles rain from above in a small radius. | Move out of the circle or tank with high defense. |
| **Petrifying Gaze** | Medusa glows purple and screams. | Turn your character's back to her immediately. |

The Petrifying Gaze is the most dangerous mechanic. If hit, you are stunned for 7 seconds, making you a sitting duck for the Homing Arrow. In the context of farming the Umbral Abyss Stone, being petrified often leads to a "reset" in your farming efficiency, as it prevents you from maintaining high DPS during her 40-second stationary windows.

<Callout type="warning" title="Threat Management">
If you are playing a squishy Mage build, keep an eye on your Threat meter. If it turns red, stop attacking for 3 seconds to let a Divine Restriction (DR) tank take the aggro. Medusa's arrows can one-shot lower-level players through walls.
</Callout>
