<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Vampire Enemies and Spawns">
Throughout Dorian's questline, you'll encounter various vampire enemies, each with specific spawn conditions and combat characteristics. Knowing how to efficiently farm them will save you considerable time.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Vampire Soldiers">
    - **Spawn**: Found throughout the vampire area, especially near the castle.
    - **Strategy**: Round them up and use AoE abilities for quick clears.
    - **Drops**: Small chance for Pure Blood Vial, Nocturnal Thread, Crimson Essence, and Shadow Infused Iron.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vampire Elites">
    - **Spawn**: Appear after killing enough Vampire Soldiers; a message "Stronger vampires can now be seen around the castle" will pop up.
    - **Strategy**: Similar to soldiers but tougher. Focus fire or use crowd control.
    - **Drops**: Same as soldiers, plus a chest with potential runes.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="info" title="Corrupted Vampire Lords">
The Corrupted Vampire Lord is a key target for the later stages of the quest. It spawns after defeating several regular Vampire Lords. Look for a double message indicating both the Primal Werewolf and Corrupted Vampire Lord spawn. They have a distinct purplish aura and no shield below 50% health.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Progression to Elder Vampire">
The ultimate goal of the vampire questline is to become an Elder Vampire, one of the best races for mages due to its significant stat boosts and passive HP regeneration. This involves both infection and crafting the Bloodbound Relic.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Get Infected">
    Find an Elder Vampire player to bite you. There's a 5% chance of infection, leading to the "Scarlet Affliction" trait for 24 hours. If you die, the infection failed. You can only attempt infection every 12 hours (or 6 hours with Golden Clover Crop).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Obtain Pure Vampire Blood">
    This mythical item drops from Corrupted Vampire Lords (from their chest). It's a rare drop but essential for progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Use Pure Vampire Blood">
    Once infected and with Pure Vampire Blood, consume it. You have a 70% chance to become a Fletchling, 29% for a regular Vampire, and 1% for an Elder Vampire.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Craft the Bloodbound Relic">
    This relic requires items from Dorian's daily quests (Phantom Flask, Essence Drop, Cursed Sigil) and Alchemy skill level 70. You can craft it at the pot maker.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Upgrade Your Race">
    Use the Bloodbound Relic or additional Pure Vampire Blood to advance from Fletchling to Vampire, and then to Elder Vampire. You can craft the Bloodbound Relic twice.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Elder Vampire Benefits">
The Elder Vampire race offers +20 Intelligence, +10 Dexterity, +20 Constitution, 5% more HP, +1 Speed, 10% magic damage, 2.5% HP regen every 5 seconds, poison immunity, and 15% more mana with +3 flat mana regen. It also grants the "Heightened Senses" ability.
</Callout>

</Section>
