<Section icon={Target} title="Advancing to the Capital: Shadow Hand and Artemis' Tower">
Once you've cleared the initial Grasslands quests, the main questline will lead you to the Clover Capital. Here, you'll delve deeper into the overarching narrative, dealing with the mysterious Shadow Hand organization. The Capital introduces more complex enemies and multi-stage objectives. You'll need to interact with NPCs in the Capital to gather information and receive new tasks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Investigate Shadow Hand">
    Upon reaching the Capital, your first task is to ask around for information regarding the Shadow Hand. Speak to the designated NPCs within the city.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Eliminate Shadow Hand Goons">
    You'll be tasked with defeating several Shadow Hand goons. These enemies typically use void magic and can be found outside the Capital, usually to the left of the main entrance. Prepare for tougher combat encounters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ascend Artemis' Tower">
    The quest will then direct you to Artemis' Tower. Thankfully, a fast travel option is available for a small fee (around 300 bucks), allowing you to bypass the climb. Speak with the NPC at the top for further instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat Wind Mages">
    At Artemis' Tower, you'll need to defeat 15 Wind Mages. These enemies are usually found near the tower's vicinity and are part of tracking the Shadow Hand's activities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Void Magic Enemies">
Shadow Hand goons often employ Void magic, which can be challenging. Consider adapting your combat strategy or magic type if you find yourself struggling against them.
</Callout>

### The Aetherstone Golem:
To defeat the Aetherstone Golem, you first need a Rune, which drops from Bandit Lords or Warlords. Once you have a Rune, you can find other players to tackle the Golem. The Golem drops a Corrupted Mana Crystal, a key item for the quest.

<Callout type="tip" title="Aetherstone Golem Strategy">
The Aetherstone Golem is a group encounter. It is highly recommended to team up with other players to increase your chances of success. Focus on high damage output and coordinate attacks.
</Callout>

### The Devil Fight:
The final major challenge in the current main questline is defeating a Devil. This fight can be extremely difficult, especially if the Devil is a Tier 2 variant. Preparation is key, including crafting potions and ensuring your gear is up to par.

**Preparation for the Devil Fight:**

| Item Category   | Recommendation                                         | Notes                                                    |
| :-------------- | :----------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------- |

| **Weapon**      | Abyssal Staff (from Kraken tokens) or equivalent       | Significant damage boost required                        |
| **Armor**       | Crafted armor with good stats (e.g., INT, CON)         | Reduces incoming damage, increases survivability         |
| **Teamwork**    | Highly recommended for Tier 2 Devils                   | Coordinate with others, especially for debuffs/DPS       |
| **Stats**       | Ensure balanced INT and CON for mage builds            | More INT than CON for buff (if applicable to your build) |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Exploring Tundra and Desert: New Challenges and Artifacts">
The main quest will eventually lead you to the Tundra and Desert regions, introducing new environments, enemies, and complex artifact collection quests. These areas often involve tracking down specific NPCs and engaging in unique mechanics like mana collection totems.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Find the Owl in Tundra">
    After consulting with the Capital's advisor, you'll be sent to the Tundra to find an Owl (an NPC, not an actual bird). This NPC is crucial for understanding the corrupted mana situation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect Corrupted Mana">
    The Owl will provide a Mana Collection Totem. You'll need to place this totem near corrupted Yetis and strafe them around it to passively collect 100 corrupted mana. This process typically needs to be repeated several times.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Place Runes at Entrances">
    Next, you'll be tasked with placing runes at various entrances, usually bridges or key points in the Tundra. Follow the quest indicators to locate these spots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate the Tundra Maze">
    Enter the Tundra Maze (located in the Tundra Ruins) and find a specific artifact (a rose) within its depths. This maze can be tricky, so pay attention to the path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Confront the Vulture in Desert">
    Your journey then takes you to the Desert Town to find the Vulture. This NPC will guide you through a series of quests involving desert scorpions and other challenges related to the corrupted mana.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Tundra Maze Navigation">
The Tundra Maze can be confusing. Look for a secret path near the crystal cave where lesser devils spawn. Following the mini-map can also help in navigating this area.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Endgame Preparations and Gauntlet Challenges">
As you approach the current end of the main questline, you'll encounter bosses like Camson and the Kraken, leading up to the final Gauntlet challenge. These quests often involve repeatable tasks to farm materials for powerful gear upgrades.

### The Camson Encounter:
You'll eventually be tasked with defeating Camson. Camson is an event spawn, meaning he's not always present. He typically spawns in a specific cave (path: right, left, right, left, right to the entrance). Team up if needed, as he can be a tough opponent. After defeating Camson, you'll charge the Sands of Time by collecting mana from corrupted Alpha Scorpions.

### The Kraken Hunt:
The Kraken is another significant boss. You'll need to defeat the Kraken and collect five Kraken tokens. These tokens are essential for crafting the Abyssal Staff, a powerful weapon. The Kraken has a daily quest and a repeatable quest, making it a good source of XP and tokens.

### Ice Golem and Gauntlet:
The main quest also involves defeating the Ice Golem, often requiring a Greater Mana Crystal as a prerequisite for group attempts. Finally, the questline culminates in reaching Wave 13 of the Gauntlet. This challenge prepares you for upcoming content, such as the Desert Dungeon.
