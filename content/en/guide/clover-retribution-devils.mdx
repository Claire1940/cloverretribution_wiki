<Section icon={Map} title="Devil Rifts, Tiers, and Spawn Mechanics">

Finding a devil involves monitoring world events. There is a base 1% chance for a Devil Rift to spawn during any major event. However, you can significantly increase these odds by participating in specific world activities. Sacrificing Mana Wisps is the most reliable method, with each wisp adding 1% to the spawn chance, capping at a 20% bonus.

Rifts are color-coded based on the devil's primary stat. While you can reroll these stats later using a Devil Eye, finding the right color early can save you resources.

| Rift Color | Primary Stat | Buff Provided |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Purple** | Intelligence | Magic Damage increase |
| **Red** | Constitution | Damage Reduction and HP |
| **Orange** | Strength | Bludgeoning Damage increase |
| **Yellow** | Dexterity | Slashing and Piercing Damage |

Rifts also come in four tiers. You should always aim for Tier 3 or Tier 4 rifts, which are distinguished by black lightning flickering around the portal. Tier 1 and 2 devils are generally considered "fodder" because they have lower level caps (200-300) and can only be upgraded once.

<Callout type="tip" title="Tier Identification">
Always look for the black lightning. A Tier 4 devil can reach Level 600, providing 1,200 total stats if it is Corrupt. Tier 1 devils cap out at 600 total stats, making them significantly weaker in the endgame.
</Callout>

| Devil Tier | Spawn Chance | Level Cap | Despawn Timer |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | 58% | Level 200 | 10 Minutes |
| **Tier 2** | 29% | Level 300 | 15 Minutes |
| **Tier 3** | 12% | Level 400 | 25 Minutes |
| **Tier 4** | 1% | Level 600 | 35 Minutes |

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Bound vs. Unbound Contracts">

When you enter a rift and confront a devil, you are presented with a choice: forge a contract or fight. This is the most critical decision in your **clover retribution devils** journey. Making a Bound Contract is easier but comes with permanent, debilitating penalties. 

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Bound Contract">
    - **Pros**: Instant access to 50% of devil stats. No combat required.
    - **Cons**: Permanent debuffs (EXP loss, HP reduction, or Gold drain). Capped at 50% Union.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Unbound Contract">
    - **Pros**: Access to 80%+ of devil stats. No permanent debuffs. No Union cap.
    - **Cons**: Requires defeating the devil in a 1v1 boss fight.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

If you choose the Bound route, you must select a sacrifice. These penalties remain even if you store the devil in the game pass storage.

| Sacrifice Type | Penalty Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Offer Potential** | 50% loss to all EXP (Level, Magic, and Weapon) |
| **Offer Wealth** | 50% loss of all Gold earned |
| **Offer Life** | Permanent 25% reduction of your Max HP |
| **Offer Soul** | Permanent Soul Drain debuff (slowly destroys soul) |

<Callout type="success" title="The Golden Rule">
Always choose the Unbound Contract. The combat is difficult, but the lack of permanent debuffs and the higher stat ceiling make it the only viable choice for serious players.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Union Mastery and Chibi Evolution">

Once you have acquired your devil, you must progress your Union Mastery. This is a measure of time spent in Union Mode (activated by pressing T). It takes approximately 50,000 seconds (13.88 hours) to fully max out this bar.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Union">
    Start using Union mode. At this stage, mana consumption is high (10 mana/sec) and you have no visual indicators.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Chibi Unlock (7 Hours)">
    At the 25,000-second mark, your devil shrinks into a Chibi form that floats over your shoulder. You can now level the Chibi separately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Face Marks (10% Union)">
    Upon reaching 10% Union Percent, your character gains distinct face marks, and mana usage begins to scale down based on mastery.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Max Mastery (14 Hours)">
    At 50,000 seconds, you unlock Horns in Union mode. Mana usage is reduced to its minimum (5 mana/sec).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Tip">
Do not push your Union Percent past 9% until you have maxed your Mastery. If you reach 10% Union with low Mastery, the mana drain will be nearly double, forcing you to grind twice as many soul fragments.
</Callout>

Feeding your Chibi lesser devil parts (Hearts, Eyes, Horns) increases its level. At level 5, the Chibi unlocks a permanent passive trait.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Devil Personalities and Meditation">

Your devil's personality determines how you earn Devil XP and what questions it will ask during meditation. To identify your devil's personality, enter Union mode and wait 11 minutes. The devil will begin whispering messages.

| Personality | Identification Message | Quest Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vengeful** | "They should suffer" / "Forsake mercy" | Kill bosses alone / Kill players |
| **Mysterious** | "Will you delve deeper?" / "You must explore" | Complete high-level quests / Event bosses |
| **Arrogant** | "You don't need anyone else" / "Not enough souls" | Kill bosses alone (No Party) |

Meditation is the primary way to increase your Relation with your devil. You must meditate (press N) for 10 minutes straight to trigger a conversation. Correct answers grant +3 Relation.

<Callout type="info" title="Meditation Answers">
Correct answers change once you pass 30 Relation. Always ensure you are providing the specific response your devil's personality demands.
</Callout>

| Personality | Question 1 Answer | Question 2 Answer (30+ Rel) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vengeful** | "Reduce it to ash" | "Expose and break the traitor" |
| **Mysterious** | "In the heart of night" | "Step with purpose" |
| **Arrogant** | "Your name, mighty one" | "Who earns loyal hearts" |

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Soul Farming and Stat Optimization">

To reach the maximum potential of your **clover retribution devils**, you must feed them Stat Souls. These items increase specific stats and replenish devil mana. Intelligence (Wisdom Souls) is the current priority because devil spells like Hellflame Torrent scale exclusively with the devil's Intelligence stat.

| Soul Type | Primary Stat | Drop Sources |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Soul of Wisdom** | Intelligence | Shadow Hand Goons, Alpha Wolves, Gauntlets |
| **Soul of Fortitude** | Constitution | Stone Guardians, Desert Claws, Gauntlets |
| **Soul of Valor** | Strength | Ogres, Yetis, Gauntlets |
| **Soul of Nimbleness** | Dexterity | Wolves, Lesser Devils, Gauntlets |

<Callout type="success" title="The Perfect Stat Spread">
For a Tier 4 devil, aim for 450 Intelligence and 150 Constitution. If you have a Corrupt devil, you can push Intelligence as high as 600, resulting in massive damage output for your Union spells.
</Callout>

Finally, you can craft powerful equipment using devil drops. The "Sin" series of weapons requires wings, tails, and teeth dropped from high-tier devils. These weapons provide mana burn effects and bonus damage against light creatures (Spirits).
