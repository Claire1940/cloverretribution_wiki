### Crafting and Upgrading Armor

Crafting plays a significant role in obtaining and improving armor in Clover Retribution. Many early to mid-game armor pieces can be crafted, and the quality of these crafted items can be exceptional if you utilize certain game mechanics.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Materials">
    Collect ores and other crafting materials from mines and enemy drops. For example, the Bandit Lord area is a good source for various materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find a Crafter">
    Locate NPCs who can craft gear. The starting area often provides access to basic crafters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Dwarves (Optional but Recommended)">
    If you have the Dwarf race or a Mythic switch, transform into a Dwarf before crafting. Dwarves provide a significant boost to crafting success rates and the potential for higher quality items (Exceptional, Superior). This can turn a 60% success rate into 80% or more.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade Your Armor">
    Once you have your desired armor, use items like Monoc Crystals to upgrade its stats and further enhance its effectiveness.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success">
The Dwarf race is incredibly underrated for crafting. Using a Dwarf can vastly improve your chances of getting exceptional quality armor, giving you a considerable advantage early on.
</Callout>
