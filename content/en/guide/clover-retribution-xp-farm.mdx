<Section icon={Trophy} title="Top Locations for XP Farming">
Effective XP farming revolves around identifying and consistently completing activities that yield high experience points. These locations and activities offer the best return on your time investment in Clover Retribution.

### Vampire Castle (Arctic Wasteland)

This location is a goldmine for repeatable quests. By talking to Dorian, you can pick up quests that reward a significant amount of XP.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Dorian">
    Travel to the **Vampire Castle** located in the Arctic Wasteland.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Accept Quest">
    Speak with **Dorian** to accept the "Defeat Vampire Elites" quest. This quest offers a base of **35,000 XP**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm Elites">
    Defeat the required number of Vampire Elites. With XP buffs, this can easily translate to **130,000 - 140,000 XP** per completion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Repeat Daily">
    This is a daily quest, making it a consistent source of high XP. Return each day to maximize your gains.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### World Bosses: Crimson and Kraken

World bosses offer massive one-time or repeatable XP rewards, making them crucial for burst leveling.

### Utilizing Dragon Ball Wishes

Clover Retribution features a unique Dragon Ball system, allowing players to summon powerful dragons for wishes, including substantial XP boosts.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Dragon Balls">
    Gather all **7 numbered Dragon Balls** of the same type (1-7).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Summon a Dragon">
    Summon one of the three available dragons: Pyrrhix, Verdagon, or Maligon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Wish for Strength">
    When prompted, select the wish: **"I want to be stronger!"**
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Receive XP Boost">
    Each dragon grants a different level of XP, Magic Mastery, and Weapon Mastery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Dragon                 | XP Reward | Magic Mastery | Weapon Mastery |
| :--------------------- | :-------- | :------------ | :------------- |
| **Pyrrhix, The Ember Scale** | 12,000    | 10,000        | 10,000         |
| **Verdagon, The Forest Guardian** | 35,000    | 18,000        | 18,000         |
| **Maligon, The Corrupt Wraith** | 50,000    | 30,000        | 30,000         |

<Callout type="tip" title="Dragon Ball Efficiency">
Save your Dragon Ball wishes for critical leveling points or when you need a quick boost to reach a new content threshold. Maligon offers the highest rewards, making it the most desirable.
</Callout>
