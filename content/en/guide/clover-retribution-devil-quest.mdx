<Section icon={ScrollText} title="Understanding Devil Contracts">

There are two primary ways to seal a deal during the **clover retribution devil quest**. Your choice significantly impacts your stat distribution and the "price" you pay for power.

| Contract Type | Stat Gain | Requirement | Sacrifice Options |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bound** | 50% of Devil Stats | Level 150+ | EXP, Max HP, Gold, or Soul Drain |
| **Unbound** | 80% of Devil Stats | 1v1 Victory | None (No debuffs applied) |

### The Sacrifice of the Bound
If you choose a Bound contract, the devil will demand a portion of your essence. This results in a permanent debuff while the contract is active. You can choose to sacrifice your Gold gain, your maximum health, or accept a "Soul Drain" effect. Most players opt to sacrifice Gold if they have a reliable farming method, as HP and EXP debuffs can hinder late-game progression.

### The Trial of the Unbound
The Unbound contract is the "true" path for meta-chasers. By defeating the devil in a 1v1 duel, you bypass all debuffs and gain a massive 80% of their stats. This is the only way to maximize your build efficiency, but it requires precise blocking and dodging.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
If you are not using a **Divine Restriction** build, ensure you have mastered the game's parry mechanics before attempting an Unbound battle. One mistake can lead to an instant reset.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Mana Management and Soul Fragments">

Maintaining your Union state requires **Devilish Mana**, represented by the purple bar. Once you finish the **clover retribution devil quest**, you must farm Soul Fragments to keep this bar filled.

| Item | Mana Recovery | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lesser Soul Fragment** | 50% | Orcs, Mercenaries |
| **Soul Fragment** | 25% | Monks, Wolf Hunters, Alpha Wolf |
| **Greater Soul Fragment** | 35% | Shadow Hoods, Ogres, Stone Guardians |

Beyond simple mana recovery, specific **Souls** can be used to permanently increase your devil's stats:
- **Soul of Wisdom**: Increases Intelligence (Blue/Purple).
- **Soul of Valor**: Increases Strength (Orange).
- **Soul of Fortitude**: Increases Constitution/HP (Red).
- **Soul of Nimbus**: Increases Dexterity/Speed (Yellow).

<Section icon={Flame} title="The Hellfire Devil Encounter">

During certain world events, you may encounter the **Hellfire Devil**. This is a unique encounter that requires specific tactics to survive. Unlike the standard **clover retribution devil quest** targets, Hellfire uses environmental hazards.

**Hellfire Attack Patterns:**
- **Hellfire Explosion**: A massive AOE centered on the boss at the start of every phase.
- **Meteors**: 12 homing meteors that track the player's movement.
- **Hellfire Tower**: Summons pillars of fire under your feet; keep moving to avoid continuous burn damage.
- **Homing Bullets**: Rapid-fire projectiles that trigger once the boss falls below a certain HP threshold.

<Callout type="warning" title="Battle Strategy">
The Hellfire Devil only takes significant damage during its "Down Phase." Focus 100% on dodging during the attack phases and save your high-damage cooldowns for when it stops moving.
</Callout>
