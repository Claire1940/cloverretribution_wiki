<Section icon={Zap} title="Permanent Stats and Four-Leaf Grimoire Pity">

The most popular reason to engage with the **clover retribution prestige** system is the permanent stat gain. Players can prestige up to five times to gain a massive boost to their base attributes. Each time you prestige using the "Another chance at life" option, you receive 2.5% of your current level as permanent stats. At the current level cap of 405, this translates to roughly 10 points per stat category (Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence).

For those hunting the elusive Four-Leaf Grimoire, the prestige system offers a structured pity mechanic. Your chances of success are directly tied to your level at the time of the reset.

| Level at Prestige | Base Four-Leaf Chance | Fail Pity (Next Attempt) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 200** | 5% Chance | No Pity Bonus |
| **Level 405 (Cap)** | 10% Chance | Increases to 20% |
| **Level 405 (2nd Fail)** | 20% Chance | Increases to 25% |
| **Level 405 (3rd Fail)** | 25% Chance | Increases to 30% |
| **Level 405 (4th Fail)** | 30% Chance | Increases to 40% |

<Section icon={BookOpen} title="Legion Magic and New Spells">

The 2026 update introduced Legion Magic, a tactical magic set based on chess pieces. This magic is highly effective for players who have just completed a **clover retribution prestige** because it allows for stationary defense and healing while you are at low health.

**Legion Magic Pieces:**
- **Pawn**: Attacks and heals the closest target. You can summon multiple pawns to create a defensive perimeter.
- **Rook**: Performs a large AOE attack and provides a shield based on 10% of your Intelligence.
- **Knight**: The primary damage dealer. It provides a 30% damage buff to nearby allies.
- **Bishop**: A high-cost healing piece that can eventually cast Death Ward at higher mastery levels.

Additionally, the new Blood Magic spell, **Crimson Torrent**, has become a staple for survivability. Obtained via a scroll from the Blood Witch, its damage is proportional to the healing it provides. This makes it an excellent tool for staying alive during the dangerous level 1-100 grind after a prestige reset.

| Spell Name | Source | Mana Cost | Key Feature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Crimson Torrent** | Blood Witch Scroll | ~100 Mana | Damage scales with healing |
| **Chrono Burst** | Time Magic | Varies | Now acts as an evasive move |
| **Legion Knight** | Legion Magic | High Drain | 30% Damage Buff for allies |

<Callout type="tip" title="Mana Management">
Legion Magic pieces continually drain mana while active. If you run out of mana, all your summoned pieces will disappear. Invest your first few stat points into Intelligence to keep your summons active longer.
</Callout>
